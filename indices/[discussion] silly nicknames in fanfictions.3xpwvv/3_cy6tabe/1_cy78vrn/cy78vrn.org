Most of my friends nicknames are related to their names, usually finding a two syllable version of it ending in a vowel whether it's their first or last name.

Elizabeth -> Lizzie, Effie.

Jonathan -> Jono.

Nicolas -> Nicky.

Ernest -> Ernie

Charles -> Charlie

Ginevra -> Ginny

Hermione -> Hermy
