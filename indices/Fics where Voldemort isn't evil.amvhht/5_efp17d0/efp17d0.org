Here are a few long fics that you might enjoy.

- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11540013/1/Benefits-of-old-laws][Benefits of Old Laws]] - When Voldemort gets a new body at the end of the Triwizard Tournament he regrets the madness in which he led his alies to decimate wizarding society. He returns to his original goal of protecting the magical world, and he pursues this goal with magical innovation and intelligent political maneuvering rather than violence.

- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10311215/1/October][October]] - Harry, as Death personified, changes the future after meeting 11 year old Tom Riddle on the train to Hogwarts. This Tom Riddle eventually embarks on a new path, never pursuing the misanthropic goal of becoming Voldemort.

- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4025300/1/Reverse][Reverse]] - Hermione is pulled into an alternate universe in which the roles of Dark and Light magic are switched. Tom Riddle was the leader of a failed Light rebellion against a tyrannical Dark regime led by Dumbledore. (Riddle doesn't become a major character until Ch 25.)
