Some good fics with adult protagonists:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11697407/1/Contractual-Invalidation][Contractual Invalidation]], linkffn(11697407): Head Auror Harry Potter uses Daphne Greengrass to advance his agenda and career.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5511855/1/Delenda-Est][Delenda Est]], linkffn(5511855): Harry time travel to 1975, teams up with Bellatrix.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11922116/1/Gothic][Gothic]], linkffn(11922116): Postwar Harry hires Ginny as his assistant for some 'adventures'.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13111277/1/Harry-Potter-and-the-Secret-of-Atlantis][Harry Potter and the Secret of Atlantis]], linkffn(13111277): Postwar Trio search for Atlantis as treasure hunting curse breakers. You might want to read its prequel [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13052802/1/Petunia-Evans-Tomb-Raider][Petunia Evans, Tomb Raider]], linkffn(13052802), that focuses on Petunia, Sirius, Bill, and Fleur.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11265467/1/Petrification-Proliferation][Petrification Proliferation]], linkffn(11265467): Dumbledore takes the Basilisk more seriously, and basically solves the plot with Amelia Bones. It's refreshing to see how slightly more competent adult good guys can affect the story.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13045929/1/Reformed-Returned-and-Really-Trying][Reformed, Returned, and Really Trying]], linkffn(13045929): 'Reformed' Grindelwald breaks out of Nurmengard to avenge Dumbledore and to help Harry fight Voldemort.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6331126/1/Strangers-at-Drakeshaugh][Strangers at Drakeshaugh]], linkffn(6331126): Harry and Ginny's work/life in 2009.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/7502511/1/The-Sea-King][The Sea King]], linkffn(7502511): Beautiful Harry/Hermione one shot set 19 years later.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11858167/1/The-Sum-of-Their-Parts][The Sum of Their Parts]], linkffn(11858167): Postwar Harry and his friends declare war on the incorrigible Ministry and Wizengamot.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4356667/1/Wish-Carefully][Wish Carefully]], linkffn(4356667): Lucius Malfoy reflects on Voldemort's 'Pureblood Utopia'.
