Correct Way To Make Tea:

1. Squeeze Lemon into Cup
2. Add Water
3. Add Sugar, at least as much as lemon
4. Add ice to brim
5. Add lemon Slice
6. Open Teabag
7. Use Teabag as decoration for proper cup of tea
8. Enjoy your superior cup of tea.
