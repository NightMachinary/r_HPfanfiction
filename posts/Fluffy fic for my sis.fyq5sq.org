Hi,

I may be a fairly experienced fanfic reader, but my sister is not.

We are both HP fans, and during the ongoing crisis she approached me with a request for HP fanfics.

For the fun/cracky ones, The Seventh Horcrux was an easy recommendation. But when she asked me about anything romance/fluffy, I realised that all the romance I read is Haphne, which is pretty awful for someone who doesn't know the fanfiction meta. (for the record, my favourite ones are To Refuse the givens and The Pureblood Princess)

So, can anyone recommend my sister (31) any beginner romance/fluffy fanfics?
