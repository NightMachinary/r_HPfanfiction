I'm in the mood to read a fic that has main characters revolting against the tyrannical leadership of Lord Voldemort (or Grindelwald)! I want there to be battles! Glory! Fighting for freedom! Ect. ect.


** Well, canon Year 7 is all about revolt against Voldemort tyranny:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11419408/1/Not-From-Others][Not From Others]], linkffn(11419408), is canon compliant and about what happened at Hogwarts that year. Ginny centric.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11463030/1/Lady-Archimedes][Lady Archimedes]], linkffn(11463030), has a super!Hermione leading the resistance. She pretty much carried the fight on her own.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6989327/1/Children-s-Crusade][Children's Crusade]], linkffn(6989327), has a Hermione from a different dimension requesting help. They went once more into the breach and trounced the other Voldemort.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9095016/1/Betrayal][Betrayal]], linkffn(9095016), is a very good Harry/Daphne in which Harry had led an effective one man campaign against Voldemort's tyranny before freeing Daphne from sex slavery.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12076771/1/Harry-Potter-and-the-Girl-Who-Walked-on-Water][Harry Potter and the Girl Who Walked on Water]], linkffn(12076771), has large amount of flashbacks about the Trio fighting Voldemort's rule mostly by themselves.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10493620/1/Ruthless][Ruthless]], linkffn(10493620), has a modified Trio (Theo Nott instead of Ron) fighting a brutal guerrilla war against Voldemort's regime.
