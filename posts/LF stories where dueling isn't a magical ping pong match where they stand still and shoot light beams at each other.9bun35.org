**** You got it (cracks knuckles):

linkffn(The One He Feared): one of the best fights in the fandom in Chapter 4

linkffn(Realignment): you'll be swimming in awesome fights in this fic, just read through the first chapter

linkffn(The Unforgiving Minute): possibly one of my favorite time-travel fics, one of the most brutal Harry's you will ever encounter. And the fastest defeat of Voldemort ever.

linkffn(The Magicks of the Arcane): its not always good on the early parts and the ending falls flat, but make it to the end of the Triwizard Tournament and you'll find so much to enjoy (Chapter 12).

linkffn(The Prisoner's Cipher): Harry was a cackling maniac that they locked up for being basically more powerful than anybody ever. Dark ending, but the battle of hogwarts memory was spectacular.

linkffn(Ectomancer): first two chapters should tell you whether you will like this fic, highly recommend.

linkffn(The Dark Lord's Equal): a bit crack, but there are two fights in Chapters 3 and 5 that I can still remember long after reading.

linkffn(Harry Potter and the Boy Who Lived) especially later on, that fight before the tournament started was spectacularly done and believable.

linkffn(The Lie I've Lived) arguably one of the best, but its mostly at the end and makes memorable use of transfiguration in the fight.

linkffn(Wind Shear): Harry pretty much has a couple tricks that he turns into utter devastation.

Hope that will keep you happy. I have more, but they dramatically lower in quality with each. Still good action, but not always decent stories.
