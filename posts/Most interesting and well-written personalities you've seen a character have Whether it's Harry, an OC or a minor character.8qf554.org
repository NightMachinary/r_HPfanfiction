Or just a nice trait that added to their depth.


** Dahlia from linkffn(the Phoenix and the serpent). Her title was the cimmerian sorceress, possibly the best female “villain” in the hp universe since the toad infestation of 95-96.

Harry in linkffn(The One He Feared). Not to spoil it, but the fusion of two characters is rather compelling.

Tom Riddle in linkffn(Harry Potter and the unlocked knowledge). Older mature Tom Riddle who was influenced by Harry as he grew.

Ginny in linkffn(The Changeling). One of the best character coming of age tales I have ever read. Only a handful of ficus I have read actually done a good job with “what if X person was sorted into Y house instead” trope, and this one is at least in the top five.

Grindelwald in linkffn(Realignment) I have always wanted to really see the level of ability that comes with being an incredibly powerful dark wizard and owning the Elder Wand. At one point he conjures a miniature sun in his hand. Dumbledore and Tom are also great in this fic, and are actually one of the better versions of mentor-student fics.

Lockhart from linkffn(Harry Potter and the prince of Slytherin) No spoilers, but mentor!Lockhart in a serious manner.

Damien from linkffn(the darkness within). It takes a special kind of character to make a Voldemort raised Harry change sides. Brothers for life.
