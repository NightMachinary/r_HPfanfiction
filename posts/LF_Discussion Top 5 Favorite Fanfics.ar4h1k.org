** In alphabetical order:

- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4586362/1/Dark-Marauder][Dark Marauder]]: James lived on Halloween'80, what did he do? Hunt down all Death Eaters with three of his best friends of course (and making sure the third one never betray them again).
- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/3557725/1/Forging-the-Sword][Forging the Sword]]: One of the best Trio interaction I've read. Maybe the only Indy!Trio fanfiction.
- Stages of Hope: When there's no hope left in the war, what you need is to travel into another dimension to find it.
- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11858167/1/The-Sum-of-Their-Parts][The Sum of Their Parts]]: If becoming Dark Lord is the only thing that will change Britain, then Harry will do it. Of course his friends will follow him.
- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10758358/1/What-You-Leave-Behind][What You Leave Behind]]: Alternate Trio, Mentor!Dumbledore, more magical Hogwarts, and a different Magical Britain. What else do you need?

Since Stages of Hope is already mentioned, my honorable mention is:

- [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9969014/1/Seasons-of-change][Seasons of change]]: What if Harry sent Dudley a muggleborn introduction book as a Christmas gift on his first year? Heartwarming scenes ensues.
