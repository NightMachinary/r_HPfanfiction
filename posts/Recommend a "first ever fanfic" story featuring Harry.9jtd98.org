:FlairText: Request

I want to send my mom a story to read (her request). She's never read fanfiction, but knows the novels pretty well. I'm a little too deep into it to look at it objectively and think of a good introductory story.

Rehash of canon is OK. It doesn't have to be inventive. Just, any adventure or fun story set at Hogwarts. Romance (or slash) OK. No: AU, crossovers, dependence on fanon knowledge, parody / crack.

My first thought was thinking Dumbledore's Army and the Year of Darkness. But, she'll want Harry. Any other ideas appreciated.


** Linkffn(Cauterize) is always my first recommendation for anyone on the fence about fanfiction. It's canon compliant, extremely well written, and is short enough to not be a slog.

If the person likes it, I'll either recommend linkffn(Forging the Sword) or linkffn(The Aurors), depending on whether they're willing to read something unfinished.

If they're STILL clamoring for more, I suggest linkffn(Stages of Hope) with the caveat that it's bittersweet.

After that, it's all dependent on their taste.

** "What happens to the characters after the series is over" seems a good place to start for fanfics so I'll copy my recs from the other thread, plus a few:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9526039/1/Out-of-the-Fire-and-into-the-Cupboard][Out of the Fire and Into the Cupboard]] - Ron and Hermione have a wedding, and hilarity ensues.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/8125531][Hogwarts, to welcome you home]] - A fluffy feels trip about Defense professor Harry.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/11968875][To Welcome You Home]] - Similar title, completely different premise. Just a short nostalgia trip.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/245007][Lost among Our Winnings]] - A series of postwar vignettes with feels and some plot surrounding the remaining Death Eaters.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4152700/1/Cauterize][Cauterize]] - Just read it. I'll let its 1648 words speak for themselves.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/7583506/1/Letters][Letters]] - Teddy finds some long-forgotten letters Harry wrote during DH in case of his death.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11317075/1/These-Cuts-I-Have][These Cuts I Have]] and [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12787674/1/Some-Cuts-Leave-Scars][Some Cuts Leave Scars]] - Everyone picking up the pieces after the war. Lots of soul-searching.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/252249/chapters/391280][War is Over]] - More plot overall than Cuts. Lots of postwar tension and Ministry bungling. Has a next-gen James Sirius sequel, [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/369727/chapters/602150][Inter Arma...]]

And if you want some next-gen stuff to go along with it, [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/3979062/1/Hogwarts-Houses-Divided][Hogwarts Houses Divided]] is always worth a read, and I rather liked [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8417562/1/Albus-Potter-and-the-Global-Revelation][Noahphantom's Albus Potter series]] (though its worldbuilding/magic system diverges from Rowling's style quite a bit in the later books).
