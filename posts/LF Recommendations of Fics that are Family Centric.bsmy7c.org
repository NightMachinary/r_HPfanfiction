As the title says I am looking for fics that are solidly family-centric. By this I mean families that raise their kids well and are not horribly dysfunctional (of course they probably aren't perfect). My favorite example of this is linkffn(Living With Danger). I've also checked out the following recent threads on this sub [[https://www.reddit.com/r/HPfanfiction/comments/b8rfed/harry_raised_by_others/][Harry Raised by Others]] and [[https://www.reddit.com/r/HPfanfiction/comments/br2hme/hermioneharry_siblings/][Hermione/Harry Siblings]] and have downloaded the ones that interest me to my Kindle. Gen or Cannon pairings, please.

Thanks in advance.

Edited to add: It doesn't have to be Harry-centric any family will do.
