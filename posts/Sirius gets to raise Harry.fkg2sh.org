I've just finished reading Innocent by MarauderLover7 for the nth time (all the sequels included) and I really want to read something similar.

Can anyone suggest some fics (preferably novel length but I'll take anything) where Sirius gets to raise Harry? (Either from when he was a baby or further along in the plot, I don't care)

** linkffn(Lost: Small Boy, Answers to Harry) is the start of a series. Sirius takes of with four-year-old Harry. I didn't care for it, so I never read any of the others.

linkffn(Homecoming by Brennus) has Harry brought up by Sirius.

In linkffn(The Son of Prongs and the Snitch and Stone), Harry is brought up by Sirius and Remus under a fidelius. I didn't care much for this one.

I quite liked linkffn(Sirius' Savior by silverfawkes). Sirius runs off with toddler Harry to Indonesia and America. There's a sequel.

linkffn(The Power of the Press by Bobmin356) has Sirius take Harry after his name comes out of the Goblet, Probably not quite what you're looking for, but has elements of it.
