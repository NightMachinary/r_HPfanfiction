I'm looking for fics where the main character learns to use magic before knowing about hogwarts or the magical world in general, like Tom Riddle did before Dumbledore's visit to the orphanage.

Examples: Linkffn(The Perils Of Innocence; Fantastic Elves and where to find them; Hermione Granger, Demonologist)


** I'm avoiding all stories where someone from the magical world meets/rescues him early, or time travel or finds his parents' trunks in the attic or something. Only the kinds where he learns on his own or from sources unconnected to the wizarding world.

My favorite example of this is linkffn(2428341) Mastermind Hunting, where Harry develops a few specific powers and then unknowingly causes some pretty major real-world history developments (the Worldwide Web and the Iraqi invasion of Kuwait for starters) before he even gets to Hogwarts.

linkffn(Thief of Hogwarts) is a funny one where he develops some unique talents, one of which is magical. Sadly dead.

Linkffn(10937871) Harry develops some magical talents before learning about the magical world in Blindness, which is a complete and pretty comprehensive fic.

linkffn(Harry Potter and the Hermetic Arts) has him developing his own magic based on legends and role-playing games. Short, but that's because it's still new.

Linkffn(Core Threads) starts out like much older fics, builds into a massive powerwank yet remains somewhat interesting. Supposedly not dead but it hasn't been updated in a while.

linkffn(Finding Why's) was curious, though the English isn't perfect and there are numerous spelling errors and mixed words. It had a unique viewpoint, though, and I found myself reading to the end despite the flaws.

If OCs aren't objectionable and you can ignore some rough writing, linkffn(12798308) gives Harry a unique power and has him develop powers well before Hogwarts.

If you like/don't mind self-insertion fics, linkffn(3252882) A matter of force is an over the top parody of such that's a fun fic anyway if you aren't expecting too much.

If you're good with crossovers, there's a Dresden Files based series that starts with linkffn(The Denarian Renegade), as Harry picks up a coin carrying a fallen angel.

linkffn(A Fallen God) starts with Loki falling off the Bifrost in the MCU, and his shredded soul accidentally gets stuffed into young Harry's head to become his mentor.

linkffn(The Wizard of the Kaleidoscope) begins with Harry developing a power from Naruto (no characters appear in the fic), and the world has elements of several universes melded with it including the Nasuverse (Fate/stay night, etc), which makes everything pretty complex but also interesting.
