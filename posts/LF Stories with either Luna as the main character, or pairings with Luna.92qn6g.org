Preferably non-oneshots, and the longer the better. Also, hopefully more than just pure romance, but if that's all, it's also fine. If the pairing is with Luna, I'd prefer Harry/Luna, definitely without Harry/Hermione (so none of that Paladeus' challenge: Champions of Lilith), and preferably fem!Harry. I guess otherwise I'd like for no Luna/Hermione, or Luna/any Death Eater or Professor, because that's creepy. I also prefer f/f pairings, but given the lack of Luna fics overall, f/m can do.

I've already read linkao3(Tomorrowland by winterfool), linkffn(Unspeakable Beauty; Luna's Hubby), the story where Luna befriends Snape, the story where she kills the Death Eaters, plants roses, and is somehow the Child of Prophecy, along with the Black Princess series, which despite basically being fem!Harry/Luna, should not count. I've also read some other well-known fics that I can't recall their names right now, along with a vast number of one-shots.

Thanks in advance!
