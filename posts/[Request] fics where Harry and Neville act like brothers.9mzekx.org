"Where a Potter goes a Longbottom shall follow."

I have been craving a good Harry and Neville brothers-in-arms fic for a while now. I don't mind fanon "Ancient and Noble" houses and politics. I only have hard restrictions on Harry ending up with Ginny and ANYONE with Snape/Malfoy.

I prefer HHr, but like Haphne, Honks, Luna, Susan Bones, and Fleur. I'm only really against Ginny.

(Also Snape is too obsessed with The Idea of Lily Evans to be woo'd, and Draco's just a racist shitbag.)

I'd prefer each fic recommendation to be greater than 100k words as shorter than that are either poignant one shots or read too quickly for me.
