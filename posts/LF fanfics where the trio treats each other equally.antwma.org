No Ron bashing, no Super!/Smart!/Independent Harry and preferably no Hermione bashing. Ron's not a jerk, Hermione isn't a perfect know it all and Harry isn't completely clueless.


** Harry and Ron take some time to get up to speed in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11910994/1/Divided-and-Entwined][Divided and Entwined]], linkffn(11910994), but eventually all three of them become awesome in their own ways.

From the same author, [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13111277/1/Harry-Potter-and-the-Secret-of-Atlantis][Harry Potter and the Secret of Atlantis]], linkffn(13111277), casts the postwar Trio as adventuring curse breakers. It's a Harmony fic with a quite charming Ron. You may want to read its prequel [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13052802/1/Petunia-Evans-Tomb-Raider][Petunia Evans, Tomb Raider]], linkffn(13052802), to have a better idea of this AU.

Harry still takes the lead in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11858167/1/The-Sum-of-Their-Parts][The Sum of Their Parts]], linkffn(11858167), but the way the three of them care for each other is very moving.

[[https://www.tthfanfic.org/Story-30822][Hermione Granger and the Boy Who Lived]] tries to retell the canon plot in a non magic sci-fi universe. IMO, it's wildly successful. The Trio is a lot more awesome in this fic than their canon version.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6989327/1/Children-s-Crusade][Children's Crusade]], linkffn(6989327), is a highly underrated dimensional travel. A Hermione from another world asks for help, and the Trio and Ginny once more enter the breach and kick some serious asses.
