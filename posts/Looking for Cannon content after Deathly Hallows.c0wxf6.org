** Hogwarts Houses Divided is a GREAT story about Teddy Lupin's first year at Hogwars and a serious reckoning between the Hogwarts houses. It was written before some of the later revelations came out (hence Teddy is a Gryffindor in this story while JKR would later confirm that he became a Hufflepuff), but it sticks to the canon as told in the books, and the tone and feel is very close.

While the main characters apart from Teddy are mostly OCs (but really well-written OCs), most of the surviving characters from the books make at least a cameo. Harry gets a fairly substantial role as Teddy's godfather, Ron and Hermione get a few moments, and Neville is Head of Gryffindor. linkffn(3979062)
