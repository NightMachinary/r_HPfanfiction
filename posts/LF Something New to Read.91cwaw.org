After all, rereading the same stories over and over again is only fun for so long. Anyways, I'm looking for something long to read, something that'll have my attention for a while. My only requirements are that it not be a time/dimension travel story, a Slytherin!Harry story, and that it not have H/Hr, Drarry, or Harry with any professor or adult who is more than 8 years older than him.

I don't really care if it's het or slash. The only pairings I really prefer are H/G, Harry/Seamus, and H/Multi (the female version), but those are optional.

And for reference: I've read The Firebird Trilogy & The Bonds of Blood by Darth Marrs, most of The Harem War by Radaslab, most of Vance McGill's newer works, the Backwards With Purpose series, Sleepless at Hogwarts, Blood and Chocolate by Lady Lemon Drop, and a few other things I can't list off of the top of my head.

Thanks! ☺️
