Hey guys, I've been on the hunt for fics featuring our favorite characters living life post Hogwarts while still interacting heavily in the Muggle World. For example, Harry could potentially reconcile with the Dursleys (or just Dudley) and Hermione would be balancing her life with her parents and the magical world while Ron learns how the Muggle world works what with plugs and batteries. I always thought it would be interesting to read how others imagine what the three would do and if they'd break off from the Muggle world completely.

Looking for a fic that's hopefully Harry x Hermione but that's unnecessary. One of my favorites of this genre (minus the Ron bashing) would be linkffn(12265183)

Thanks!
