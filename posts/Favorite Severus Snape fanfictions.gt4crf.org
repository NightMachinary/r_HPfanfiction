And because I know someone will (probably/maybe) request this: linkffn(11923164) This is one of my favorites, as well.

I love Severus Snape, flaws and all. Yes he was a bully to children, and that isn't okay, but he's such an interesting character to me (always has been), I love characters that have flaws, they make them more complex.

So I'm curious, what stories are some of your favorite Severus Snape? It can be as Canon or as AU. It can be Muggle or Magical. It can be Time Travel, Traveling to another AU, or anything else.

I don't want him to be a cuddle bunny, I would like for him to be similar to canon, even if he has more of a development with certain characters like Harry, Sirius, Remus or whoever else.

Snape can be a main character, a secondary character, a side character or a background character, not particular picky on that.

Would rather NOT have Weasley, or Dumbledore bashing, if at all possible.

If Harry is in the story: If Harry is smarter and/or more powerful then in Canon, I'd like for it to be believable, because he worked for it.

For Pairings (If any): Am fine with: Het/Slash/Femslash. Threesomes/Bisexual/Asexual, Et Cetra. (Doesn't have to focus on pairings).

Can be: Oneshots/Drabbles/Multichapters. Can be on ANY site, Anyone can be the main character. Can be an older or newer story.

Would rather it be completed or regularly updated, but if not that's fine.
