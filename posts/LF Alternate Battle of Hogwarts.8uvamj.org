*** Here are some fics with different battles of Hogwarts that stand out to me:

- The Prisoner's Cipher linkffn(The Prisoner's Cipher): told through memories, but it is one of my all time favorite depictions of battle in the wizarding world. You will know it when you get there.
- Harry Potter: Wizards at War linkffn(Harry Potter: Wizards at War): a tropey fic, but it does have a large scale battle of Hogwarts with everything from elementals, teleporting, vampire armies, acromantula hordes, and founder inheritance.
- Harry Potter and the Sword of the Hero linkffn(Harry Potter and the Sword of the Hero): there is a final battle between the death eaters and aurors at the end, with hundreds battling all at once. The sequel /also/ has a Battle of Hogwarts, with another Voldemort (its complicated).
- Abraxas linkffn(Abraxas): sort of a super!Harry fic, Harry and Ginny are both overpowered and trap the death eaters in a pincer move while Dumbledore distracts Voldemort. Honestly though, I like this fic because what happens after is very different from what you would expect.
- A Champion's New Hope linkffn(A Champion's New Hope): a bit dated, but the final Battle is pretty detailed and its reasonably entertaining to read (its multiple chapters).
- Prince of the Dark Kingdom linkffn(Prince of the Dark Kingdom): a massive AU, this fic has two battles of Hogwarts, though the last one is far more amazing, and Voldemorts dialogue during and after are really funny ("Harry, for once you have managed /not/ to misplace yourself").
