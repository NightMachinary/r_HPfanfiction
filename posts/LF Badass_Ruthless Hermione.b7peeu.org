** This is easily my favorite subgenre. In no particular order:

linkffn(Lady of the Lake) Dramione. Hermione & Draco plot a political take-over with a twist.

linkffn(Divided and Entwined) Hermione leads the muggleborns in a revolution against Voldemort and Pureblood supremacy. Hermione/Ron with shades of Harry/Hermione/Ron.

linkffn(Six Pomegranate Seeds) Trippy Time-travel but not plot. Easily my favorite read of the past year. Hermione as Other as she plots on how to save the world from disaster while maintaining her sanity and preserving the timeline. Just read it.

linkffn(Stages of Hope) A suicidal and high Harry, a ruthlessly determined Hermione, an alternate universe and the best Sirius Black characterization I've ever read. Bring tissues.
