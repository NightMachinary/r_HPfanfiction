** Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Summer of Change) Harry x Tonks. Lots of people say it hasn't aged well but I still enjoy it. It's word count is longer than the first three HP books and it's complete so it might be what you're looking for.

Linkffn(Jamie Evans and Fate's Fool) Another Harry x Tonks. This one's about the length of Philosopher's Stone, and involves time travel/dimension hopping, and fem!Harry.

Linkffn(2841153) Harry Potter and the Sword of Gryffindor. This one's Harry x Hermione. It's a smutty parody fic but it's well written and I found it hilarious.

Linkffn(5010568) In Too Deep. This one's Harry x Hermione, where Hermione was raised by Death Eaters and goes to Hogwarts as Voldemort's spy. Don't remember too much about it, but it's in my favorites.

Linkffn(The Philosopher's Stone Retransmuted) This is another Harry x Tonks, and the first entry of a series of rewrites (called the R:Series). Follows canon until OotP: Reassembled, then completely diverges from it (as in the author doesn't rewrite HBP but uses completely original ideas). I recommend it even though I had to stop reading after OotP:R. I think I'll read the rest eventually. It's also complete.

Linkffn(Son of Potter, Daughter of Black) This is another fem!Harry x Tonks, but it's still in progress and hasn't been updated since 2017. The author claims it's not abandoned. But it's a good read other than male!Harry's animagus form and it's 280k+ words.

Linkffn(The Life He Leads) Another in progress Harry x Tonks fic, but I think this one's abandoned. Interesting enough read, though.

The shortest one of the list is Jamie Evans, but that one's about the length of Philosopher's Stone. The longest is the R:Series as it rewrites 5 books then carries on for like 3 more stories after that (possibly more). Hope you enjoy some of these.
