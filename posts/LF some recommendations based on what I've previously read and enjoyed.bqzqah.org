** If you like action, I recommend pretty much everything by Starfox5.

Linkffn(Divided and Entwined)

One of my favorites: While Harry and Ron get trained by Dumbledore and Moody in sixth year to defeat Voldemort, Hermione runs a guerilla resistance group to hunt Death Eaters because the Ministry isn't doing anything. All three see plenty of action.

Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Lady Thief)

Another one of my all time favorites, this one has less battles (only a big Voldemort battle with Harry, Ron, Dumbledore, Malfoy, Voldemort and some battles around Azkaban and against Crouch) but plenty of sneaky heist scenes.

Linkffn(Petunia Evans, Tomb Raider)

Petunia didn't marry Vernon and settle in a normal life. When she couldn't go to Hogwarts, she settled for studying archeology and exploring ancient tombs. Then she gets the offer to work with the Gringotts curse breaking team....

Linkffn(Prologue to a Revolution ; Hermione Granger and the Marriage Law Revolution)

Plenty of action, but mostly around Neville, Ginny and Ron, as Harry and Hermione have to run the country.
