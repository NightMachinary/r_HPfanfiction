* Harry more powerful than Voldemort
:PROPERTIES:
:Author: OliverBellwood
:Score: 14
:DateUnix: 1562417237.0
:DateShort: 2019-Jul-06
:FlairText: Request
:END:
I'm looking for a fiction where Harry is really superior to Voldemort. None of that equal-in-power/lame-but-lucky/slightly-less-powerful-but-training bullshit. I'm looking for a fic where Harry and Voldemort meet and Voldemort realizes that Harry could wipe the floor with him. I sometimes read Tomarry and in a majority of fics, Harry and Tom are either equally powerful or Tom becomes Harry's mentor. Even when Harry and Tom are supposed to be equal, Tom is the only one capable of incredible feats of magic (and is the most magically-cultured) while Harry struggles to turn a matchstick into a needle. So I want a powerful Harry with power rolling in waves and either have Tom in awe or Voldemort afraid


** Hmm. Linkffn(gods amongst men) voldy realizes that Harry is quickly becoming equal to him and Dumbledore and will surpass them soon. Linkffn(Learning to breath) has a powerful Harry that's already beat him and faces something a little worse. Linkffn(The Sun source) is a crossover with something I don't recognize but he's pretty OP and more powerful than voldy. Linkffn(The mind arts) he's on his way to being on par with voldy.
