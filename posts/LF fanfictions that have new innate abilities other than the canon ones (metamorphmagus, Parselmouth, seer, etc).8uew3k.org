There is a small number of individuals referred to as "aethers" who have the ability to interact with magic depending on a particular sense (sight, touch, and sound all occur here; its sort of like magical synesthesia).

linkfffn(Prince of the Dark Kingdom) Harry has and/or gains multiple powers as time progresses, including magical sensing and a form of animancy, not including his more "moon" based powers.

linkffn(Blindness) Harry's disability actually grants him a form of mage sight.

linkffn(Barefoot) Harry basically has psychometry here.

linffn(Ectomancer) Harry gains the ability to access the spiritual world, mostly through pools of water on the ground
