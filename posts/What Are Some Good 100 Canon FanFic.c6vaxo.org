I'm looking to reread the original books and I thought I might read some fan fiction along with them. What are some good ones that can be read with the books that are completely canon?


** [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5677867/1/Ginny-Weasley-and-the-Half-Blood-Prince][Ginny Weasley and the Half-Blood Prince]], linkffn(5677867), is a great Year 6 fic from Ginny's POV.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11419408/1/Not-From-Others][Not From Others]], linkffn(11419408), is IMHO the best Year 7 Hogwarts fic, also from Ginny's POV.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5486257/1/Grave-Days][Grave Days]], linkffn(5486257), happens immediately following the Battle of Hogwarts.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4152700/1/Cauterize][Cauterize]], linkffn(4152700), is an excellent one-shot set a few years after the Battle. Its 1648 words pack a lot of emotion.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13096373/1/In-Case-of-Emergency][In Case of Emergency]], linkffn(13096373), is quite a juicy postwar Harry/Ginny smut fic with a decent plot.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6331126/1/Strangers-at-Drakeshaugh][Strangers at Drakeshaugh]], linkffn(6331126), is perhaps the best postwar canon compliant story. It depicts Harry and Ginny's work/life in 2009.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12925628/1/Interview-with-Director-Potter][Interview with Director Potter]], linkffn(12925628), is a little self promotion. I wrote this one-shot for the occasion of Battle's 20th anniversary.

There's also a non-compliant alternate ending that I liked, where Voldemort chose to defeat Snape instead of killing him, thus leading into a post-war hunt for the remaining Death Eaters, and settling down to life afterward.
