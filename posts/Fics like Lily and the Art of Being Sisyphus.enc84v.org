Basically as the title says, I'm looking for trippy fics like Lily and the Art of Being Sisyphus.

Fem!Harry preferred but not required. Doesn't even need to be Harry protag at all.


** [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/14760663][Blackpool]], [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/13719279][Still Life with Skull]], and a few others of TheDivineComedian's works have a similar sense of unreality that Lily and the Art of Being Sisyphus has.

Idk if you've read any of The_Carnivorous_Muffin's other fics, but [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/15675435][October]], while more serious, has a lot of the same elements.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/6997183][the train station]] by dirgewithoutmusic is about Ginny and is similar in that it is very surreal.
