Something that drives me crazy in the actual books and many fanfic is that the Taboo on Voldemorts name isn't explained until too late. Harry about to say his name, and people stop him, and instead of saying "hey, if you say his name, death eaters will show up" there is just a bunch of screaming about it.


** I think in linkffn(The Arithmancer; Lady Archimedes; Annals of Arithmancy) it's a detection system based on the country-wide rune stone network that's also responsible for the Floo Network, the Trace and a few other things controlled by the ministry. Only once Voldemort has control over the system he can add a simple alarm, if someone says a specific word, in this case his name, it sets off an alarm and logs the location. It's his name because nobody except the Order uses it and they don't get many false alarms that way.
