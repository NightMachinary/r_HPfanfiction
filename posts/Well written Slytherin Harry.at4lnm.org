* Well written Slytherin Harry
:PROPERTIES:
:Author: Edin19
:Score: 12
:DateUnix: 1550764031.0
:DateShort: 2019-Feb-21
:FlairText: Request
:END:
I would prefer ones where the kids don't act like politicians at the age of eleven, and without the whole Ancient Noble house of etc etc.. (Unless it's done well)

Good examples of what i'm looking for are fics like these; Sitra Ahra, Fraterculus and Hogwarts Battle School. DrizzleWizzle's series are quite similar as well, in regards to what i'm looking for.


** /Black Luminary/ has Harry sorted into Slytherin but he was adopted into the Black family at a young age. Politics come into play, but I think it's handled really well. The story itself is pretty AU, as a heads up. linkffn(12125300)

/Harry Potter and the Accidental Horcrux/ has the horcrux in Harry's head waking up and essentially raising him. linkffn(11762850)

/On the Way to Greatness/ is easily the best Slytherin!Harry story ever written. It reads like a character study and you can easily see how small everyday choices compound to turn Harry into what he eventually becomes. linkffn(4745329)

/Victoria Potter/ is currently being worked on and just started second year. It's a Fem!Harry story but Taure does a great job keeping the magical world magical. linkffn(12713828)

/What Would Slytherin Harry Do/ shouldn't really be taken too seriously as it's a humor fic first and foremost, but I found it enjoyable. linkffn(3559907)

Honorable mentions:

I enjoyed both /Just Another Orphan/ and /Death Before Dishonor/, but they aren't up to par with the above stories. They were also abandoned before the stories really got anywhere. linkffn(10511318; 10724650)
