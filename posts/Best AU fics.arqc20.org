Preferably in the Marauder Era, but really anyone will do, I just seem to have a thing for HP AUs


** [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10937871/1/Blindness][Blindness]], linkffn(10937871): Because Harry was blind, he never went to Hogwarts. But he had his own superpower.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4586362/1/Dark-Marauder][Dark Marauder]], linkffn(4586362): James survived, and went on a rampage with his Marauder buddies.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12614436/1/Hermione-Granger-Demonologist][Hermione Granger, Demonologist]], linkffn(12614436): Lonely Hermione learnt to summon demons as friends.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11265467/1/Petrification-Proliferation][Petrification Proliferation]], linkffn(11265467): Dumbledore took the Basilisk a little more seriously, and the canon plot was shot.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13052802/1/Petunia-Evans-Tomb-Raider][Petunia Evans, Tomb Raider]], linkffn(13052802): Petunia Evans became an ass kicking archeologist, but she still had her issues. Fortunately, Harry and Sirius were there for her.
