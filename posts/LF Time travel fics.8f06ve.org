I am looking for time travel fics. Before or during First Wizarding War. I would prefer ones with pairings. I have read Wind Shear recently. My only disappointment was it didn't have that much romance. Also I am loooking for Harry as main character. And please don't recommend fics that are uncompleted and hasn't been updated for months.


** /To Deny Defeat/, Cracky. Harry is upset that Malfoy beats him at Quidditch so he goes back in time to prevent it from happening. linkffn(3847381)

/Timely Errors/, Harry gets sent back to his parents time. Hints of Super!Harry, although it isn't overt. linkffn(4198643)

/Welcome to Hogwarts 1949/, Harry goes back to a time where Tom Riddle is still at school. It's /alright/, although I prefer /Past's Player/. linkffn(2550563)

/Delenda Est/, Harry ends up a teacher at Hogwarts during Voldemort's first rise. The beginning is well executed, although after a certain event happens the story turns tedious. Harry/Bellatrix. linkffn(5511855)

/The Wizard from Earth/, Harry gets sent back a couple thousand years. Him trying to make sense of a time well before modern civilization. linkffn(8337871)

/Past's Player/, Same premise as /Welcome to Hogwarts 1949/ although I think Riddle's characterization in this one is better executed. linkffn(5736901)

/Stepping Back/, Harry goes back as a student during his and the Marauder's sixth year. He ends up telling everyone that he is James' twin brother. linkffn(12317784)

I could recommend more, although they would be time-travel within a more pronounced AU.
