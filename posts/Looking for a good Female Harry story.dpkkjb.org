Lately I have been reading a lot of Harry Potter fanfictions and came across a few that Harry was born female or turned into a female. I'm just wondering if you wonderful people know any other fics to read.

Thank you in advance for your help.


** I have several fics with a fem!Harry in one form or another (YMMV):

- linkffn(Victoria Potter): possibly the most canon-like fem!Harry story out there, currently in Year 2. Slow update rate but well worth the read.
- linkffn(A Long Journey Home): one of the most interesting time-travel fics out there as fem!Harry has to slowly work her way back to the present across millenia. The Merlin arc is particularly great.
- linkffn(Potter vs. Paradox): this one is more so-so, but its still entertaining.
- linkffn(Iris Potter and the Goblet's Surprise; Tales of Three): a pair of fem!Harry fics with a more abrasive MC personality, but fairly well-written if a bit over the top.
- linkffn(Jamie Evans and Fate's Fool): a bit of an odd fic, but complete and with some entertaining throwdowns with Grindelwald (I think I remember Godzilla being mixed in there somewhere).
