I'm looking for famous, well-written fics like KwanLi's Hogwarts Battle School, or Kurinoone's Dark Prince Trilogy. I'd appreciate anything really because I'm literally going crazy in quarantine.

I'm not against slash, but I'd prefer not reading it. Or at the very least, minimal amounts of slash which is not relevant to the plot, along with a warning.

Just so you get an idea, I didn't like HPMOR, and Prince of Slytherin. POS mainly because I didn't like the way it's written, what with it's premise, as well as the fact that it takes *forever* for it to get anywhere.


** Well, I don't anything about famous fics but I got some good long fics I can recommend.

Quoth the Raven, Nevermore - linkffn(6652537) - A slower start in the beginning but the pace picks up for sure.

Make A Wish - linkffn(2318355) - A long fic that is plenty of fun and anything by [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/686093/Rorschach-s-Blot][Rorschach's Blot]] is plenty long and a good read.

Daphne Greengrass and the Importance of Intent - linkffn(13133746) - One of the best Harry and Daphne fics out there and has good read and reread quality. Its sequel is just as good. A little shorter than 100k+ but still plenty long.

Harry Potter, Squatter - linkffn(13274956) - A good crossover story that great for killing some time and is very touching.

From Southampton to New York and Beyond - linkffn(12300252) is another great story and the author [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/8431550/Dorothea-Greengrass][Dorothea Greengrass]] a good few other interesting stories to read all of the being plenty long.

Novocaine - linkffn(13022013) - A well thought out and interesting world-building story that also delves more into the magical culture.

Heroes Assemble! - linkffn(12307781) - A great Marvel x Harry Potter crossover that is really well done.

Dragon Chronicles 1: Muggle-Raised Champion - linkffn(11610805) Is also a good story done by the same author.

Not sure if these are anything you like, but most people seem to like these. Not sure if any of these are famous.
