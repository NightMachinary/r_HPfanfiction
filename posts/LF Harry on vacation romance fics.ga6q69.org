A few weeks ago I browsed through here and stumbled upon the fic "From Southampton to New York and Beyond" by Dorothea Grengrass and I really enjoyed it.

Since then I've been craving for stories similar to it, simply put I'm looking for fics where Harry goes on vacation and gets away from all of the drama.

I would like to have romance in it but having a wholesome story where Harry explores the world and has the time of his life is great too.

My favorite pairing is Harry x Daphne but I'm up for any pairings you guys have.

I'm really open minded so anything you got I'm down to give a chance!


** I got you, I love the vacation trope. I honestly wish there was more, it is so hard to find any.

The best of the best vacation trope fanfic in my humble opinion is

Make A Wish - linkffn(2318355) - It is like my top 5 favorite stories of all time and the world that is created from this is amazing.

Finding a Place to Call Home - linkffn(9885609) - Is sort of like Harry taking a vacation, more like escaping Britain but he does treat it like a vacation of sorts.

Albus and Harry's world trip - linkffn(13388022) - Another interesting traveling story.

The Stag and the Flower - linkffn(13132670) - Harry/Fleur taking a vacation together and falling in love.

The Lost Summer - linkffn(8554615) - A very nice fic about Harry vacationing in France.

And these are all I have time to share, hopefully I can share more later
