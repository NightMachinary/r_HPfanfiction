#+TITLE: LF fics that have new fields of magic

* LF fics that have new fields of magic
:PROPERTIES:
:Score: 28
:DateUnix: 1528917286.0
:DateShort: 2018-Jun-13
:FlairText: Request
:END:
Fields of magic that are unique or aren't in canon but can still fit in the books and are believable. Or something briefly mentioned in Canon but never explored (Alchemy, Ancient Runes, anything in the Department of Mysteries). Or a fic that expands on a field of Canon (transfiguration, charms or potions).


** Okay, let the games begin:

- The One He Feared linkffn(The One He Feared) - explores numerous magics, including curse breaking, magical artifacts, and more symbolic magic
- Dodging Prison and Stealing Witches - Revenge is Best Served Raw linkffn(Dodging Prison and Stealing Witches - Revenge is Best Served Raw) - goes into particular depth to necromancy, divination, and the animagus transformation
- The Firebird Trilogy linkffn(The Firebird's Son: Book 1 of the Firebird Trilogy) - goes into heavy detail about divination, where its not a joke like it is in canon.
- Magicks of the Arcane linkffn(Magicks of the Arcane) - deals with a number of magics, including runes, protective enchantments, ancient magic, and the Black Arts (summoning demons)
- C'est la vie linkao3(C'est la vie) - Harry is an expert in ancient runes, goes into heavy detail of the subject
- Pureblood Pretense linkffn(Pureblood Pretense) - I believe she is a potions savant, but its been a while
- The Art of Self-Fashioning linkffn(The Art of Self-Fashioning) - Harry is a transfiguration master, and is eventually able to take the mastery to an unreached level (no spoilers)
- By The Divining Light and Conlaodh's Song linkffn(By The Divining Light; Conlaodh's Song) - old magic is a strong part of the series, and Harry's method of using it is practically unique
- Ectomancer linkffn(Ectomancer) - Harry is diving through puddles and exploring the more spiritual side of magic
- Prince of the Dark Kingdom linkffn(Prince of the Dark Kingdom) - pagan magic is a huge part of this fic, but there are other magics that are somewhat unique, including Harry displaying a gift for animancy

Thats all I can think of at the moment.
