** Ooh~

Linkffn(The Red Dragon by Yes I am using a typewriter) has around ninety-something chapters. It's a time travel fic where harry goes back to the body of his younger self. It also has a slightly altered canon and Gryffindor! Draco. It's currently on hiatus, but it takes a bit to read and it was mostly impeded by things happening in the authors life, so with quarantine, who knows, you might just get an update. The current pairing is Harry x Luna if I remember correctly.

Linkffn(Red Bolt Of Lightning) is shorter but has many sequels and an intriguing plotline. It's a bit tropey, but it's good! There's also little-to-no bashing. The pairing is Harry x Female OC, but the OC is well written to my memory.

Linkffn(Brother in Arms by SteamGeek01) is a creepypasta crossover, but it's got 93 chapters, well developed characters, and readers are allowed to suggest things and give input. Last time I checked, the author has writer's block, but again, it's quarantine, so you might get an update. And in the meantime, there's a non-canon extras book to check out as well. There are lots of pairing, mostly slash, but the main one is Harry/Isaac x Ben Drowned. It will make sense in time and I've grown to love the ship.

Oh and linkao3(Death is but the Next Greatest Adventure) has 55 long chapters and is well-written. It's got MoD!Ravenclaw!Harry and the pairing is Harry x Tom. But not in a pedophilia way, or something. It's more focused on Tom rather than Voldie, because there's a difference in this fic.
