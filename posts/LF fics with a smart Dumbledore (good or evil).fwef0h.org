All the evil and good Dumbledores in fanfics tend to be morons getting outsmarted by little boys and girls who still have their milk teeth. That's kind of boring to read.

And infuriating, if the author insists on telling us how he's totally so smart even as he pulls a Three Stooges routine.

So are there any fics where good old Albus actually /shows/ his intellect and skills, and actually feels like a mastermind?

Goes double for an evil one, actually.
