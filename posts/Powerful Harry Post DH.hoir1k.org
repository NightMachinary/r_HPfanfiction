** He's become very powerful, but he's not Dumbledore in [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/6334630/chapters/14514247][The Sum of Their Parts]], linkao3(6334630).

He's shown to be very powerful, but he's mostly relegated to a desk job apart from some earlier actions in [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/6273811/chapters/14375362][Hermione Granger and the Marriage Law Revolution]], linkao3(6273811). He has to let professional soldiers fight at front, and he doesn't like it.

He's also shown very powerful, when he butchered 8 would be assassins in rapid sequence, but he's mostly manipulative in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11697407/1/][Contractual Invalidation]], linkffn(11697407).

He's certainly very powerful, when he and Sirius together fought Voldemort to a standstill in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/3401052/1/][A Black Comedy]], linkffn(3401052), in order to save Bellatrix.

He's become very powerful, as he rules their fledgling interstellar colony with an iron fist in the only completed, plain vanilla, very flawed, "Wizards in Space" fic [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/7135971/1/][The Voyage of Starship Hedwig]], linkffn(7135971).
