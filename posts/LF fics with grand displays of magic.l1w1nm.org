Like, not necessarily OP! fics, but fics where majorly powerful acts of magic are performed.

Stuff like how in “The Prince of Slytherin” ([[https://archiveofourown.org/series/1119027][AO3 link]], linkffn(11191235)) Voldemort and Dumbledore are extraordinarily powerful (ex: Voldemort's actions in the Diagon Alley memory (massive transfiguration of the burning pitch, making it rain snakes, etc) and Dumbledore's aqua veritas transfiguration), or how one of Grindelwald's battles created a void where no wanded magic can occur

Or the sort thing Grindelwald's forces could do in “Divided and Entwined” (linkao3([[https://archiveofourown.org/works/11645676/)]]) - stuff like (I'm just mentioning some stuff that happened in the past of the story, so I wouldn't consider it a spoiler, but someone else might) raising entire cities of inferi or releasing magical plagues

Please no “evil Harry” fics. If he uses “dark magic” or is morally gray that's fine, but not absolute evil. Also please nothing that is too heavy in bashing - a little bashing is fine, but not an excessive amount.
