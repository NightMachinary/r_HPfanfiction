Just look at canon, we have the Fidelius Charm which can hide places (and presumably information) in a soul, basically everything about the Sword of Gryffindor, a Jinx/Curse that makes it so no instructor of a specific course will continue onto a second year in that course, a mother's protection that keeps a child safe from magical threats just by living with a mutual blood relative, potions that make someone lucky and successful in what they do and spells placed on a location that remind muggle's of important errands they need to run elsewhere.

Yet most of what we see in fics is stupidly large pyrotechnic crap. So I'm looking for a fic or fics that feature subtle magic. Curses that cause someone to have increasingly bad luck over a period of time, and so forth.

Things I have already read:

What You Leave Behind by Newcomb

The Lesser Sadness by Newcomb

Lesser Evils by ScottPress

The One He Feared by Taure

Forging the Sword by Myst Shadow

Harry Potter and the Untitled Tome by Ihateseatbelts

The Shadow of Angmar by Steelbadger

Ectomancer by RustyRed

Any help would be appreciated.
