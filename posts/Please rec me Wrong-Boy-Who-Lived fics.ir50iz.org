preferably where Harry is neglected, but his parents regret and try to reconcile with Harry.


** Not a lot that fit your second criterion that are worth reading.

In general, *Harry Potter and the Boy Who Lived* is the cream of the crop. *Subversion* is a personal top-20 fic that is sadly unfinished and definitely doesn't get enough attention.

*Prince of Slytherin* and *Sarcasm and Slytherin* are similar, somewhat long-winded WBWL and Slytherin Harry fics with good storytelling but a good deal of tropes and mediocre writing. *To Be a Slytherin* is WBWL-ish, but is competently written even if it drags a bit.

*Harry Potter and the Wizarding World* is a decent guilty pleasure. *The Saga of the Lightning Speaker* is an exceptionally mediocre guilty pleasure.

*Antithesis* and the *Sacrifices Arc* are often recommended by others, but they weren't for me.

That's really all I can recommend.
