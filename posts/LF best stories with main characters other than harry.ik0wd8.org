** I'd like to recommend several Ginny centric fics:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4740107/1/][Ginny Returns]], linkffn(4740107), is the only completed lone Ginny time travel

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5677867/1/][Ginny Weasley and the Half-Blood Prince]], linkffn(5677867), is Year 6 from her POV. Canon compliant

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11419408/1/][Not From Others]], linkffn(11419408), is Year 7 from her POV. Canon compliant

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6160345/1/][My Slytherin Harry]], linkffn(6160345), is from her first person POV. This Ginny is powerful, sexy, and slightly sadistic.

"The Changeling Series" by Annerb, linkffn(6919395;12569750;12713008;13143812), features a Slytherin Ginny.
