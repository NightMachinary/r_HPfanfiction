** Depends on how long you really want:

- linkffn(Harry Potter and the Boy Who Lived): pretty likely you've already read this, but Harry educated at Durmstrang as the NBWL twin.
- linkffn(Magicks of the Arcane): Harry and Dumbledore team-up and go adventuring in chapters 12-19ish, and its where the fic is frankly its best. The writing certainly improves over the course, don't worry.
- linkffn(What You Leave Behind): in one summer Harry travels to a quidditch camp in Australia I think. Pretty epic actually.
- linkffn(The Mind Arts): doesn't happen till later (4th year) but they travel to Beauxbatons for the tournament. Probably dead too.
- linkffn(Bungle in the Jungle: A Harry Potter Adventure): are possibly the standout, along with its sequel, for the development of a different magical culture and history. If the 2nd person bothers you, try using the ff app to listen to it (I think it helps).
- linkffn(Prince of the Dark Kingdom): mainly of western europe beginning at the end of fourth year, but bear in mind this is also a fairly AU fic as well.
- linkffn(Dodging Prison and Stealing Witches - Revenge is a Dish Best Served Raw): this happens quite frequently in this book, and despite the flaws in the premise the author keeps me going through shear uniqueness and new ideas.
- linkffn(Harry Potter and the Forests of Valbone): this has only culture as a tangent, but the gist is Harry gets lost Hatchet-style in eastern europe with only a car, a hat, and his wand.

And thats not including all the crossovers and so forth with Harry in entirely different worlds with his unique take on the matter.
