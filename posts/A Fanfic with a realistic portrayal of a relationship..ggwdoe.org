Hey everyone!

I am looking for Fanfics in which the relationship between Harry and the other (no slash) is realistic and fit Harry's characteristics and age.

Basically, I don't want a Fanfic where 11 enter an adult relationship and talk about marriage and such or that they have zero fights or commit to life at such a young age.

Thanks!


** Grow Young with Me - linkffn(11111990) - One of the more realistic take of relationships. One of my favorites.

The Little Child to Lead Him - linkffn(3123443) - Alos a good story about Harry trying to figure out romantic and parental relationships.

Daphne Greengrass and the Importance of Intent - linkffn(13133746) - Is another well-done story that portrays the romantic relationship well.

Picnic Panic - linkffn(12265183) - Does this pretty well as well.

Ice Cream - linkffn(13323518) - Shows off what a young relationship is like pretty well. It's quite well done.

Hope these are what you are looking for, enjoy!
