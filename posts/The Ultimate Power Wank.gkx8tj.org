We've seen Harry getting (virtually) unlimited magical power in linkffn(11118965). We've seen him conquering Magical Britain in linkffn(11858167). We've seen him so ridiculously over-powered that Voldemort is little more than a nuisance to him in fics like linkffn(10685852) and linkffn(10937871), and we've seen him (or her) take over the black market in linkffn(9486886). We've even seen him as a mad scientist in linkffn(8551180).

But what if we /combine/ these tropes?

It could manifest as basically anything- a Self-Insert (possibly as an OC), a crackfic (most likely), a one/two-shot, or even a lengthy fic as the protagonist takes over the entire world. The two closest fics to this prompt I've seen so far are linkffn(10136172) or linkffn(10972919).
