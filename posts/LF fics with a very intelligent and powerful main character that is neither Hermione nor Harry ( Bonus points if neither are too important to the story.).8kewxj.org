Maybe I have just read too many HP fics in my life. I read The Changeling some time ago and found it very refreshing to see the canon-time through (Slytherin)Ginny's eyes, but Harry still got on my nerves.

I would love to see that time period changed through someone else. Maybe Hagrid gaining some ancient giant powers or maybe Draco being Dobby's son and therefore the uber-Elfenlord of Death or maybe Tom Riddle accidental time traveling and having to kill his alter ego or maybe just Ron being the real hero.

Don't mind my ramblings, you know what I mean. Thank you!

I forgot to add: No crack please and the inner action is kind of more important to me than what actually happens.
