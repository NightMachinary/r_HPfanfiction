I read this really wicked fic yesterday where Harry, Hermione, Ron, Ginny, Luna, Neville (and eventually George) travel back in time to right before first year. They essentially finish everything by fourth year where no one dies, they became animagi, become an awesome group like the Marauders where they can help their own actions. Do you know of any fics similar to this?

*I am not looking for people other than Harry going back in time (like just Draco) and I'm not looking for the following ships: Harry Potter/Tom Riddle | Voldemort, Harry Potter/Hermione Granger, Harry Potter/Daphne Greengrass, and Harry Potter/Ron Weasley

*I am, however, okay with the following ships: Harry Potter/Draco Malfoy, Harry Potter/Ginny Weasley, canon ships, Remus Lupin/Sirius Black

Thank you for any recommendations!
