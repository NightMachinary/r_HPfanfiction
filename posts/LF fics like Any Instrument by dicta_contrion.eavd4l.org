I love the premise of this fic and the extent to which it delves into a discipline. I'm interested in more biomagical fics but would also be into fics about wandlore or Arithmancy, anything with a lot of theory and detail about a magical or magical adjacent topic.

I usually go for Drarry but happy to entertain other pairings if the fic is really good. Also, I usually prefer 100k or longer.


** Well, I haven't read "Any Instrument", but here are two series. The first one is about Arithmancy with Hermione as the main character. She's paired with George. It's complete- all 7 years+ post- Hogwarts. Hermione has some really good character development throughout, but beware Umbridge has a really bad case of plot armour in Year 5.

The second one is about Potions and has a female Harry with no pairings (so far). It's still ongoing and is up to the middle of Year 4. I really love it and think it's very well written.
