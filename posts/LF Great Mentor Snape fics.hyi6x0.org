I'll take anything from Snape mentoring Harry or Hermione or the trio together(or rly anyone haha) to Snape as a somewhat uncle or father figure.

** This is my guilty pleasure trope, so I've got several recs.

I think one of the best father figure Snape fics is the [[https://archiveofourown.org/series/14893][Redefining Life series]] by OliverSnape. The first story is The Definition of Home linkffn(5878880)

The [[http://www.potionsandsnitches.org/fanfiction/viewstory.php?sid=1663][Finding a Family and a Home series]] by Hestia includes mentoring the trio, Neville, and Draco. It is easiest to read on Potions and Snitches because of the piecemeal way it was posted on FFN. Those smaller FFN stories were later aggregated into two stories. linkffn(4360923; 4486541)

Other favorites:

- The Best Revenge and sequel Time of the Basilisk by Arsinoe de Blassenville is a good mentor-turned-father figure fic. linkffn(4912291; 5843959)
- Harry's New Home by kbinnz linkffn(4437151)
- One Good Turn by Empathic Siren. To my knowledge, not on ffn or ao3. Can be found on [[http://www.turn-of-the-page.com/atotp/others/empathicsiren/onegoodturn.html][A Turn of the Page]] (login required) or [[http://thetwobroomsticks.slashcity.net/siren/OneGoodTurnindex.html][The Two Broomsticks]].
- The Shaun Snape series by TeacherBev: Snape's Invisible Friend, Growing Up Snape, and Shaun Snape, Age 8. linkffn(2690841; 2734902; 3018371)
- The Mine Series by Gillian Middleton: Mine, Mine 2, Snape's Vocation, The Owl and the Puppy-Dog, Fossils and Frog Spawn, The Absence of Unhappy, After All These Years linkffn(1963825; 1969019; 2311797; 4525721; 2398928)
