Just something that's written so good that a character death or an event can make me cry or turn emotional. Something like the death of Katie In A cadmean Victory or Harry's first girlfriend In the Refiners Fire.


** Luna's stories in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6892925/1/Stages-of-Hope][/Stages of Hope/]] made me feel some feelings so I highly recommend that.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11261838/1/The-Unwinding-Golden-Thread][/The Unwinding Golden Thread/]] also hits hard at certain points, one of my favorite stories by The Carnivorous Muffin.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12933896/1/Things-you-cannot-leave-behind][/Things You Cannot Leave Behind/]] is a long-shot but it is truly an exceptional story. Definitely go through it one sitting.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6167455/1/Things-Unsaid][/Things Unsaid/]] isn't really a story, but some parts are just particularly poignant and it's hard to not react to that.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/16044434/chapters/37454639][/we can't control (watch me unfold)/]] is exactly the type of story you're looking for. A major character death towards the end made me tear up.

Finally, [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/287028][/We Were the Potters/]] is a short one-shot, but I really enjoyed it and don't get a chance to recommend it often so here it is.
