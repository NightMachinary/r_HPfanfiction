I've always thought she was underutilized. I'm planning to have her as one of the main characters in one of my fics, but I'd love recommendations featuring her in others.


** I am a big fan of Winky as well. There are unfortunately just a few stories which gives her credit I know about:

1.  linkffn(2531438) ... this is a very strange story, one of very very few with positive Vernon Dursley; worthy of reading.

2.  linkffn(7392700) ... kind of run-of-the-mill Harmony with heavy Dumbledore bashing

3.  linkffn(8864658) ... another very strange story, with not very creative Winky and positive (but very strange) Petunia

4.  linkffn(13133746) ... good story, but Winky is just duplicate of Dobby, not much of her own personality and story; the same goes for [[http://www.siye.co.uk/siye/viewstory.php?sid=11253]], [[http://www.siye.co.uk/siye/viewstory.php?sid=129685]], and many others linkffn(11922615).

5.  linkao3(601124) ... very good story (one of the first fanfiction stories I read, which persuaded me it is possible to write a good one), Winky has a small role to take care of Ginny while she is in her seventh year.

6.  linkao3(14078862) ... Winky is unfortunately completely abused and forced to serve with the restoration of Tom Riddle. Completely unimportant (although sad) side-story

7.  linkffn(10070079) ... another side story: more or less canon-compliant (whole story is quite AU) Winky story dealing poorly with being freed by Barty Crouch Sr.

8.  linkffn(10622306) ... I am never certain, whether this story is a serious one or a crackfic, but Petunia (who turns out to be sensitive to the magic) among other things acquires desperate Winky as her house-elf. Also, worthy of reading.

9.  linkffn(11637611;13197260) ... I don't remember much about these stories but there is a lot of Winky there.

10. linkffn(12117770) ... another one where Winky becomes a Potters' elf.
