** Hermione:

[[https://www.tthfanfic.org/Story-30822][Hermione Granger and the Boy Who Lived]] is a great story if you don't mind non-magical AU. Hermione's characterization is excellent though.

She's delightfully dark in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12614436/1/Hermione-Granger-Demonologist][Hermione Granger, Demonologist]], linkffn(12614436).

She becomes a brilliant and ruthless guerrilla leader in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11910994/1/Divided-and-Entwined][Divided and Entwined]], linkffn(11910994).

--------------

Ginny:

[[https://www.tthfanfic.org/Story-27958-12/DianeCastle+Harry+Potter+and+the+Deadly+Heller.htm][Harry Potter and the Deadly Heller]] is a crossover with Buffy. Ginny gains the Slayer power during the Astronomy Tower Battle, and takes over the fight against Voldemort.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4740107/1/Ginny-Returns][Ginny Returns]], linkffn(4740107), is a time travel and Ginny cleans up things efficiently.

--------------

Voldemort:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10677106/1/Seventh-Horcrux][Seventh Horcrux]], linkffn(10677106), is technically still Harry, but I would say he's more Voldemort. He is definitely a grand prodigy, powerful, and deliciously insane.

He scored total victory in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8192853/1/Yes-I-am-Harry-s-Brother][Yes, I am Harry's Brother]], linkffn(8192853). This fic has a few issues, but once beyond that, is actually quite entertaining.

--------------

Bellatrix:

She is powerful and smart in [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5511855/1/Delenda-Est][Delenda Est]], linkffn(5511855), and Harry generally plays her sidekick in the second part (although still crucial).

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10099028/1/Murder-Most-Horrid][Murder Most Horrid]], linkffn(10099028), is an extreme AU without Voldemort. Bellatrix begins as a cynical, but competent auror/detective. She exhibits lots of intelligence and initiatives in solving the 4 major crime cases in this story.
