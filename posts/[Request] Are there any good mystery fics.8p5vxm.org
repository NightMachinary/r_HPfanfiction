I am currently looking for mystery fics. The kind that the main characters try to figure out what happen or who done it or piece clues together to solve something. I prefer plot and story over anything else. The more suspenseful the better. I am open to almost any suggestions as long as it has good writing.

And please limit the romance fics. I'm actually getting sick and tired of them being the main focal point of a story. I don't really mind romance, but it either has to be in the background or leave it alone.

It does not have to be a murder mystery (I would to read them if there are any recs). It could range from Harry solving a case to Scorpius discovering secrets from his family. It could even be a story about Bill Weasley tracking down treasure from a mysterious map. Just a good old fashion mystery novel to keep me up at night trying to puzzle out clues.

Two excellent examples of what I am looking for are listed below:

#+begin_quote
  [[https://archiveofourown.org/works/6701647/chapters/15327019][Harry Potter and the Conspiracy of Blood]] by cambangst. All the suspense and twists are also very well planned and thought out. Literally nothing is what it seems. linkao3(6701647) Status: Completed

  [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4910668/1/The-Inquisitor-s-Son][The Inquisitor's Son]] by jinxdoll is another well written murder mystery with the main character of Scorpius Malfoy. Very interesting and I highly recommend it. linkffn(4910668) Status: Completed
#+end_quote
