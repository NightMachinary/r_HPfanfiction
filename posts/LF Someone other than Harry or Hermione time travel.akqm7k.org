So many stories have Harry or Hermione time travel; Severus is another popular choice, but I've read a lot of Severus Time Travel Stories as well; it should be pretty obscure or whatever if it's a Severus Time Travel story.

No lord/Heir to x amount of things from Harry

Little to no character bashing, especially not to the Weasley's, as I tend to really like them.

Would much rather prefer it to be complete.

No Harem Harry

No Harry/Hermione or Drao/Hermione.

I am fine with Het/Slash/Femslash.

No super Seer Luna.

Other than that, go for it.
