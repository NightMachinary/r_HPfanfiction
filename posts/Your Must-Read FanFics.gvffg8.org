** Make A Wish - linkffn(2318355) - I always recommend this to new readers for a couple of reasons. One, it's a fun, plenty of humo, and not some super angst story. Two, it has the rare element of Harry traveling the world. Three, there is a lot of references made throughout the story. Some you may miss the first time, giving it plenty of reread ability.

Also, any story by [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/686093/Rorschach-s-Blot][Rorschach's Blot]] is good and worth reading for sure.

** Most of these are slash (I just fell into that pit and never crawled out) but I say these are must reads because of the quality of writing and the novelty/ingenuity these authors bring to the table, whether it be an original concept or a twist on a common trope.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/10588629?view_full_work=true][Harry Potter and the Problem of Potions]] Gen; Harry/Ginny (but its not the focus) Lovely character development, great writing, doesn't fall into any serious tropes ie Harry suddenly becoming some grey moral mastermind or Snape being overprotective and just such a bffl. Nah, things move slowly, everyone is still basically the same but slightly different. Voldemort is still evil, Snape is still kind of a dick, the only real difference is Harry loves Potions so much hes much more willing to swallow his pride if it means he gets what he wants.

[[https://m.fanfiction.net/s/3542099/1/How-I-Learned-To-Stop-Worrying-And-Love-Lord-V][How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love Lord V]] Gen (I think it was meant to be harry/voldemort but it never got there) Crack written with just the perfect tinge of solemn darkness. It's the nuclear apocalypse after all. Honestly the funniest thing I've ever read, but surprisingly nuanced characterization.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/15675621?view_full_work=true][Lily and the Art of Being Sisyphus]] Fem!Harry/Voldemort Gender bent Harry being overpowered in the best of ways. Absurdist but everyone else is normal. A little bit meta/timeywimey. I'm still not sure if I like this but I think I do. On my list because I think the author does the insane/sociopathic perspective in an interesting way.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/7034761?view_full_work=true][Teaching History is Old News]] Harry/Tom Riddle Some crack but sad if you really think about it. Alternate universe where ages are swapped around, Tom Riddle and Harry were born in the same year. Great inner voice for Tom. Nobody is a damsel in distress but also everybody is a damsel in distress. Plot disguised as crack.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/14454174?view_full_work=true][Manacled]] Hermione/Draco Angst. Spy intrigue stuff. If the war extended and the Light side lost. The most painfully poignant ending, it hurt in the best way.

Ichor by NocturneMemory Fem!Harry/Voldemort This is only available through the authors WordPress now... She received too much hate. Very passionate, emotionally charged, non-con handled respectfully, its worth scoping out.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/11640741?view_full_work=true][The Alternarieve]] Harry/Tom Riddle Harry travels back in time/alternate universe. Tom Riddle is still working at Borgin, Dumbledore never had his falling out with Grindelwald and is minister of magic. Hilarious, insecure, young and foolish baby death eaters, kind of reverse roles between Order of the Phoenix and the Death Eaters wherein Dumbledore is a dictator and Voldemort is the antihero.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/users/astolat/pseuds/astolat/works?fandom_id=136512][Astolat (author)]] Harry/Draco Just all their stuff. It's always quality.

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/16052816?view_full_work=true][Away Childish Things]] Harry/Draco Harry gets de-aged, draco works to fix him whilst learning some unknown history. Background plot about corruption in the ministry. Good character/relationship building, captures their individual shortcomings and strengths well, explores how their childhood traumas would manifest differently.
