* Need recommendation; Fanfics that don’t bash?
:PROPERTIES:
:Score: 31
:DateUnix: 1564265980.0
:DateShort: 2019-Jul-28
:FlairText: Request
:END:
I'm so tired of every fanfic I read always containing at least some form of Weasley and/or Dumbledore bashing and sometimes even Hermione bashing. I'm not picky about pairings but I'd prefer to read a story with non canon couples (in other words not Harry/Ginny or Ron/Hermione) that aren't slash and don't have any bashing of characters. My personal favorite pairing is Harry/Luna but like I said I'm not picky, I just want a good story with no bashing. Thanks!
