I recently read Ghost of a Memory by Phoenix Boy and really loved the concept of Harry hiding his identity. Does anyone know of any other fics like that? I also wouldn't mind fics where Snape hides his identity. I have no preference of pairings.

TIA!


** Lots of fics with vigilante!Harry or indy!Harry feature this:

linkffn(Taking Control): the original, and frankly best, indy!Harry fic. Well worth the read. Harry fights the Death Eaters in disguise, assisting the order.

linkffn(Dodging Prison and Stealing Witches - Revenge is Best Served Raw): Harry ages himself to galavant around the wizarding world/take up international drug smuggling.

linkffn(Harry Potter and Death's Ultimatum): mid-Horcrux hunt Harry takes on the identity of the 'Reaper.' In short, Harry + Sword of Gryffindor x Cloak of Invisibility = a lot of dead purebloods.

linkao3(C'est La Vie by cwycross): Harry dimension jumps to 4th year where Neville is the BWL, proceeds to wreck their shit.

linkffn(Deprived by Crimson Lord): tropey as hell bodyguard!Harry for Fleur Delacour, but not unenjoyable if you can manage to stop rolling your eyes long enough to read.
