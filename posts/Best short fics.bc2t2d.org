I find that most fics that are linked tend to be hundreds of thousands of words long, which is fine, but sometimes I prefer reading shorter fics, whether that be a one-shot or something that's only a few chapters long. Rec some of your favorite short fics

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/2612901/1/There-s-Always-a-Way][There is Always a Way]], linkffn(2612901): Badly hurt Harry hired a super assassin SI from America to fight a tyrannical marriage law.
