** [[https://floreatcastellumposts.tumblr.com/][FloreatCastellum]] has written a bunch of little ficlets I loved, starting from pre-hogwarts and ending at next-gen. All of them are canon compliant. (I've linked her tumblr page here, as her ao3 account doesn't have all the fics shes posted.)

[[https://www.archiveofourown.org/works/6246406/chapters/14311300][Not from Others]] is a canon-compliant retelling of the events seventh book that detailed what happened at hogwarts while the trio were out hunting for horcruxes. Ginny POV. (Completed)

[[https://archiveofourown.org/works/552828/chapters/985108][Stunning Shifts]] is a dimension-travel fic where Harry and 5 others are thrown into an alternate dimension. The story follows how the canon group handle the situation, as well as what the people belonging to the original dimension make of these travellers. I've read quite a few dimension travel fics, and this is definitely one of the better ones. The characterisations are spot on, and there's no bashing. POV's alternate between canon-harry and his counterpart. (Ongoing. Last updated on may 31st)

[[https://archiveofourown.org/series/282654][These]] are fics by lullabyknell on ao3. There are a bunch of different types of fics here, all well written, and you'll definitely find something that catches your interest.

You haven't really specified what types of fics you enjoy(ships, tropes,etc.), so the few I've given above are canon-compliant ships or just gen.

If you do mention some tropes,etc. you enjoy, I may be able to give you some more recs.
