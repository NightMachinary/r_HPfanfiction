** Less recommended fics (broad):

- What You Leave Behind linkffn(What You Leave Behind): an interesting slight AU with a magically talented Harry and some interesting lore at Hogwarts. Harry/Fleur
- Harry Potter and the Unlocked Knowledge linkffn(Harry Potter and the Unlocked Knowledge): the horcrux came alive in summer of HBP, and its probably the most amicable relationship (friends) between Harry and Tom I can remember. Harry/Katie, which is pretty abnormal in the fandom.
- The Ring of Gold and its sequels linkffn(The Ring of Gold): a pre-DH mystery fic, probably one of the better built mysteries I can remember reading for HP. Harry/Ginny, but the relationship is less important than in most cases.
- What Lies Beneath linkffn(What Lies Beneath): a fairly straight forward tightly contained plot, and one of the only decent Harry/Tracey fics I can think of.
- The Phoenix and the Serpent linkffn(The Phoenix and the Serpent): one of my favorite older fics, Harry goes on a trip to help bring down Voldemort. Very good AU world-building and character development, and in my opinion a better backstory for Grindelwald than in canon (this was prior to even HBP coming out). Its H/G, but they are mostly apart during the story.

Less recommended fics (Harry/Daphne):

- Fraterculus linkffn(Fraterculus): Harry is the brother to the BWL, but unlike in most versions he is /less/ powerful than his brother but not resentful or overly grim.
- Days to Come linkffn(Days to Come): post-DH break from canon where Harry and Daphne meet and end up becoming good friends. Downside is the magic is kept to a bare minimum.
- The Other Champion linkffn(The Other Champion): slight AU during GoF, which is interesting in-and-of itself since most H/D fics start in PS or during HBP.
- The Lies That Bind linkffn(The Lies That Bind): an H/D fic that takes a seemingly standard start point and runs the opposite direction. Warning: you will not expect the way this story goes.
