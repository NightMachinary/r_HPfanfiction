I'm looking for fanfics revolving around Hermione. I'm open to any pairings except Harry/Hermione and Ron/Hermione bc i hate those. I also really really like time travel stories and dark! and/or Slytherin!Hermione. I've read so many fanfics at this point that it's getting harder and harder to find new ones. Oh and they must be complete. That's the number one rule for me personally bc who likes abandoned but awesome fics the ending of which you'll never know, right? They can have any rating. I'm open to adult content, too.

My favourite stories are The Debt of Time, War Paint, Pet Project, Eclairs and A Good Life.

I'll really appreciate your help.


** Linkffn(Hermione Granger, Demonologist)

Summoning Demons, brutally torturing classmates, and discussing the Dark Arts with Voldemort himself, all as a firstie. Can't get much darker than that. Dark humor.

Linkffn(The Dark Lord never died)

When Voldemort got Avada-Ke-boink-ed, Lucius Malfoy took over leadership of the Death Eaters and finished the takeover of the Ministry. In an AU where he is the dictator of Britain and Dumbledore and Harry are in exile in France, Hermione grows up very differently. Hermione/Ron but Ron is very different than canon since he grew up in France and doesn't meet Hermione until they are both adults. Secret agent Hermione.

Linkffn(Hermione Granger and the Marriage Law Revolution)

Hermione takes over the ministry after Voldemort's defeat and a war worse than anything since Grindelwald breaks out across Europe. Hermione/Harry iirc but the romance is barely mentioned between all the politicking and fighting.

Linkffn(Divided and Entwined)

In an AU where Fudge tries appeasement politics against Voldemort, Hermione decides to do something against the Death Eaters herself and becomes a guerrilla war leader. Definitely one of my favorites. Also, the Irish student gets to blow things up.

Linkffn(Patron by Starfox5)

AU with pureblood traditions turned up to 11 and muggleborns being basically slaves to purebloods. This one is Hermione/Harry with a somewhat strong focus on the unusual relationship but it's still pretty good even if you don't like the pairing. Can't say too much because I'm currently reading it and only 1/3 in.

Linkffn(Returned, Reformed and Really trying)

Grindelwald comes back after Dumbledore's death. Not quite Hermione centric but she still calls for revolutions. A bit shorter and more parody/humor than the other [[/u/Starfox5][u/Starfox5]] fics.

Linkffn(The Arithmancer)

Not that much of an AU (except for wizarding academia) but it's my favorite Hermione centric fic. Hermione/George. Also just recently got the sequel finished. Researcher/inventor Hermione.

Linkffn(The Green Girl)

Slytherin Hermione, usurps Bellatrix as Voldemort's second in command. Hermione/Draco.
