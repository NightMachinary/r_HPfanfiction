** Funny enough, I pretty much only use [[https://Fanfiction.net][Fanfiction.net]] mostly because it has a good app that has dark mode reading. And I agree finding stuff on ffn is hard, but I don't mind just scrolling through the archives. Sometimes I find something good I miss otherwise.

Make A Wish - linkffn(2318355) - A fun story where Harry travels the world. Also anything by [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/686093/Rorschach-s-Blot][Rorschach's Blot]] is a good read I strongly recommend looking at his other stories.

Picnic Panic - linkffn(12265183) - My personal favorite because its really sweet and well done.

Dancing in the Green - linkffn(11703507) - A really good story, strongly recommend.

Ice Cream - linkffn(13323518) - This one is cute and I like to share it.

Daphne Greengrass and the Importance of Intent - linkffn(13133746) - One of the best Harry and Daphne fics out there and has good read and reread quality. Its sequel is just as good.

Harry Potter, Squatter - linkffn(13274956) - A good crossover story that great for killing some time and is very touching.

Inspected by No 13 - linkffn(10485934) - A good fun crack-fic that is well done. One to be shared with others for sure. Got to love that Harry is op.

From Southampton to New York and Beyond - linkffn(12300252) is another great story and the author [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/8431550/Dorothea-Greengrass][Dorothea Greengrass]] a good few other interesting stories to read.

Novocaine - linkffn(13022013) - A well thought out and interesting world building story that also delves more into the magical culture.

Heroes Assemble! - linkffn(12307781) - A great Marvel x Harry Potter crossover that is really well done.

Dragon Chronicles 1: Muggle-Raised Champion - linkffn(11610805) Is also a good story done by the same other.

And I will end this long list here, but if you liked these I be more than happy to recommend you more if you like.
