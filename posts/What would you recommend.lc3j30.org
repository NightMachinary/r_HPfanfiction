** This sounds like a lot a fun to think about. So in regards to Harry Potter, here's my list.

Beginner:

- Make a Wish - linkffn(2318355) - A fic that I think any fan of Harry Potter can appreciate. It is one of my personal all-time favorites and requires only knowledge of the books to enjoy. In truth, any of [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/686093/Rorschach-s-Blot][Rorschach's Blot]] will fall under this category though he does make references to this fic in his others often.
- Inspected By No 13 - linkffn(10485934) - A fun little crackfic that brings up an odd yet entertaining set of events.
- Amicus Protectio Fortis - linkffn(11547735) - Another story where you need no fandom knowledge and anything the author makes up for the story is explained. One of the more unique stories I know and will always remember. This story is sadly incomplete but I think is worth recommending.

Veteran:

- Dancing in the Green - linkffn(11703507) - A fun fic that only requires the knowledge that Daphne Greengrass is often given a cold personality in fics hence the nickname 'Ice Queen.'
- Novocaine - linkffn(13022013) - An excellent post-war story and is the bar I hold other post-war stories to.
- Dragon Chronicles 1: Muggle-Raised Champion - linkffn(11610805) - Another really good story.

Expert

- Daphne Greengrass and the Importance - linkffn(13133746) - One of my favorite stories that takes the fandom characters (Daphne and Astoria) and truly make it their own.

I'm going to stop there cause I need to do something else. Been fun trying to rank these.
