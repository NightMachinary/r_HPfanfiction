Linkffn(A Black Comedy) is probably the most famous example, but you have probably read it.

Linkffn(Stranger in an Unholy Land) is an old beast, but definitely features a different world.

Linkffn(I'm Still Here) is possibly a time travel fic, but both worlds are AU to canon and feature differences.

Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Defiance of the Hero) involves a lot of universe jumping, though it's prequel sword needs to be read first. Pretty action packed.

Linkao3(C'est la vie by cwycross) is unfinished, but the authors writing skill and characterization just make this stand out to me.

Linkffn(Delenda est) and it's sequel both feature dimension jumping and time travel, though your mileage may vary about half way through.
