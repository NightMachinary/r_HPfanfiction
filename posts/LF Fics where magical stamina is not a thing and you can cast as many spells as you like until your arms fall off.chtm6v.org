****** u/Taure:
#+begin_quote
  but I'm pretty sure casting magic tires you out in canon
#+end_quote

There's not one instance of it. And plenty of instances of seeing intensive magic use without any kind of exhaustion being mentioned.

E.g. in GoF we see Harry cast the summoning charm over and over again for several hours (at least 5 hours of solid casting) and he doesn't get tired from it.

The one instance that people often mention is the PoA Dementor lessons where Harry is exhausted by the effects of the Dementors. But this is in line with the effects Harry feels every time he is in the presence of Dementors, and in line with Lupin's description of a Dementor's effect, and Lupin gives Harry chocolate to remedy the feeling - a cure for the effects of Dementors.

The feeling of exhaustion is then confirmed as the effect of the Dementor (and not the Patronus Charm itself causing exhaustion) when Malfoy pretends to be a Dementor and Harry successfully uses the Patronus Charm against him. Harry notes afterwards that he felt none of the usual effects.
