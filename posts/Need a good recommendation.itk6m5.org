Need a good severitus, or mentor snape to harry fanfic. Either completed, or really long


** I normally despise these, as they usually turn Harry into a little bitch, however, there /are/ exceptions.

linkao3(Blood Magic by Gateway Girl)

This is a post OttP story (no HBP et al) but it has superb characterization. A bit of Harry/Hermione and Harry/Draco. Also Snape/Remus. Snape and Draco are not their trollish canon selves in this, but much better versions.

linkffn(Stronger than Hope)

A bit angsty in places but very well plotted and great action scenes.

linkffn(Resonance by GreenGecko)

The best Harry gets adopted by Snape story, imo. My favorite characterization of Harry (and most other characters really) of any story. Harry slowly becomes extremely powerful, however he still gets his ass kicked frequently. Really one of a kind series and very long too (1.2 mil words with the sequels).
