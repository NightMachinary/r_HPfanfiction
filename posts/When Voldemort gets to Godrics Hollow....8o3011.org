Something different. The Potters aren't there, decoys, only one is there, they escape, Sirius is there, anything. As long as it's different, and it actually shows in the story in a major way. Not something like James wasn't there, but was still killed somewhere offscreen(page?), and all it really is a few paragraphs of mystery. Like usual the more words the better, and if it's completed even more so. Also no time-travel Harry/Hermione fics. I've had my fill of them, the change has to be natural(as natural as it can be wIth how the writer is already manipulating events past the norm).
