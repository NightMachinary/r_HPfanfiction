** I quite enjoy the “Harry Potter Pokémon Master” series by Mr. Chaos. It's Wizarding Britain plus a small fragment of non-Wizarding Britain in the Pokemon world as the regions of Avalon and Little Surrey, respectively. While one of the OCs - you'll see who - gets kinda overbearing, it's all-in-all pretty good. No magic in this one, just Pokémon and all the crazy technology of the Pokémon world. linkffn(9305868) is the first in the series.

I also enjoyed Harry Mewter by Alex Ultra linkffn(4826372). Not much Pokémon in this, other than Harry being a Mew (plot twist), but it's really well written.
