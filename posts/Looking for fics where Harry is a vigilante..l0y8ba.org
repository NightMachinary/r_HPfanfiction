** A couple more that I haven't seen mentioned (also, I realized how many vigilante stories involve time travel):

Reunion linkffn(4655545) involves traumatized Harry, Hermione, Susan, and Luna all traveling back in time. Pretty much all of the death eaters mysteriously start dying.

Returning to the Start (10687059) is a Hermione PoV of a Harry that time travels back in time, and eventually goes on a mass killing spree. Hermione has trouble understanding it. The vigilantism has a major impact on the story, but the actual acts aren't the focus.

No Fate linkffn(13694344) is theoretically a time travel fic, though at 9 chapters and 84k words they are still getting set up for the ritual to do the time travel. Harry got his magic sealed and memory of magic wiped after the underage magic trial, and a now current military Harry and a broken Hermione are now working together to set up a time travel ritual while fighting against the death eater controlled Ministry.

A Black Comedy linkffn(3401052) Harry falls through the veil of death and ends up reunited with Sirius in an alternate universe. They proceed to earn money by robbing death eaters blind.
