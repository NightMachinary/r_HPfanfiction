I'm looking for fics that focus on the pairing/relationship as opposed to being mainly about the 'plot'.

I just find a lot of the time when there's a pairing I want to read most of the fic is about Harry getting stronger, or being-uber talented and stopping voldemort with relationships being essentially afterthoughts.

I'm not opposed to there being a plot, I just want fics that prominently focus on the relationship between the main pairing; The Importance of Intent by Petrificus Somewhatus or Twelve Days by TheEndless7.
