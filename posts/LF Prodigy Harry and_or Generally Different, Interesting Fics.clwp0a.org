I recently read Harry Potter and The Boy Who Lived by The Santi and was surprised and how tuned in I was to the story. Sadly with it abandoned I am in need for a new fic. Essentially I am looking for stories with a Harry Potter who is extremely advanced (or shows potential) in one or more areas of magic, but is not unrealistically so like a Merlin-incarnate or something (ex. wandlessly out pacing his teachers after a year of schooling is just....ugh). All recommendations are appreciated, regardless of pairing(I would prefer to avoid slash but I can work with it if it's done well, just isn't my thing per say) etc. as long as it is a truly good or uniquely interesting story. Although if you don't have prodigy recommendations I would read literally anything of interest! Some ideas of other recommendation types below.

To touch on HP&BWL again, I would also enjoy recommendations for WBWL or NBWL stories, again all I ask is that is a good story by your standards.

Further ahead could have spoiler for story in question, figuring out spoilers tagging and such!

Other tropes such as the Potters still living, Harry going to different Hogwarts House, different school, having brothers and/or sisters, going dark/grey, being light but not naive, different sets of friends, and more Are all very much appreciated, just please let the premise for these to happen be believable and the story be interesting, it's truly my only real requirement. I know some of these tropes have been overdone but I haven't read too much fanfiction (60 stories or so I guess) so much is new to me and I would love to see what it offered.

Thank you all for your time, I look forward to reading your recommendations!
