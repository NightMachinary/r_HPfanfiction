** - The Prisoner's Cipher linkffn(The Prisoner's Cipher): Harry is clearly struggling with his actions and is definitely evil, but he doesn't want to be and is fighting back.

- Possibly Cage and Key linkffn(Cage and Key), though it has something of the addictive dark magic trope.
