** If you like "The Sum of Their Parts", you will also love [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10595005/1/Hermione-Granger-and-the-Marriage-Law-Revolution][Hermione Granger and the Marriage Law Revolution]], linkffn(10595005), in which they burnt the old order even more thoroughly to the ground.

Another anti-marriage law fic, [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6049450/1/Harry-Potter-s-Reaction-to-the-Marriage-Law][Harry Potter's Reaction to the Marriage Law]], linkffn(6049450), is far less violent, but still very satisfying.

--------------

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10871795/1/A-Little-Child-Shall-Lead-Them][A Little Child Shall Lead Them]], linkffn(10871795), is Hermione time travel. But instead of the usual back to her first year, she traveled all the back to her two years old self. Hilarity ensued, but also heart breaking moments.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8678567/1/A-Riddled-Universe][A Riddled Universe]], linkffn(8678567), is dimensional travel in which Harry found a world with a good Tom Riddle. But there are still dangerous enemies there.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8288341/1/A-True-Daughter-of-the-Black][A True Daughter of the Black]], linkffn(8288341), is a crazy post DH AU with an extremely OOC Bill Weasley. But if you can get past that, the story itself is very entertaining.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11697407/1/Contractual-Invalidation][Contractual Invalidation]], linkffn(11697407), has a manipulative!Harry using Daphne to advance his career and agenda.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4889913/1/Hallows-and-Pathos][Hallows and Pathos]], linkffn(4889913), has the author almost kicked off the H/G dedicated site [[http://www.siye.co.uk/][Sink Into Your Eyes]].

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/7502511/1/The-Sea-King][The Sea King]], linkffn(7502511), is a beautiful one shot.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12118000/1/Wither][Wither]], linkffn(12118000), is a horrific one shot.
