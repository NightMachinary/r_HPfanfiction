Almost every fic I've read has Harry either a) op at first year or b) progressing, but progressing at a unfathomable pace. Fics which I liked were Prince of Slytherin, The Dark Arts, and Ashes of Chaos. The whole, op Harry in 5 chapters made me legit make my own Fic [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13752653/1/Harry-Potter-and-the-Mirrored-Fates]] just so that I could see how hard it is to make realistic Harry's.


** Forging the Sword, Boy Who Lived, Phoenix and the Serpent all do this well, I think.
