Looking for Post-war fics, one-shots or otherwise where one (or more) of the following happen:

- Harry and George bonding through grief (non-slash)

- Harry and Hermione friendship (not Harmony)

- The Dursleys being confronted about their treatment of Harry by not-Harry

Preferably canon ships, definitely no Draco or anything too cross-generational.
