The Constellation should be like this: Harry has quit with Ginny (or they never tried it again), but Hermione is with Ron (or not) and at some Point moves to Harry.

To give you an example: "The Best Man" by luckintheair04 has this Scenario. Hermione divorced Ron and went to Grimmauldplace.

There was a Fic on Deviantart, whichs name i cant remember, but it contained Harry was haunted by Nightmares about those who died in the Battle and accusing him for their death. With Harry is Teddy as a Baby. Hermione is with Ron, but he makes comments that furiate her and she leaves and appeared on Harrys place. She spend the night there, the next morning Ron arrives and makes his (wrong) conclusions.

"Confusing Feelings" and the Sequel "Moving Forward", by midnight 17516 have this scenario, although its Questionable why they move into a Flat, instead of living in Grimmauldplace.

I would prefer, if Harrys place is the Grimmauldplace, but it doesnt have to be.

I would be glad, if the Fics start after the War, but im fine with "epilogue-compliant" Adult Harry and Hermione.
