Looking for fanfics with Harry as the main character no slash, any hetro pairing (just my preference) except Harry and Ginny! I'm going to be bed ridden for a couple months because I was hit by a car and I need something to read while I'm in bed! Long fics preferred! Thanks!

Edit:

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11317075/1/][These Cuts I Have]]/ is Harry/Ginny, so avoid that one. Sorry about that.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11762850/1/][HP and the Accidental Horcrux]]/ has the horcrux in Harry's head waking up and essentially raising/guiding him. It is done a lot better than other stories I've read with the same premise. It also has a sequel that is currently on hiatus. No official pairing, Harry is implied to be asexual in this.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10210053/1/][HP and the Untitled Tome]]/ is fairly AU, Dumbledore & Grindlewald's famous duel ended in a stalemate and this explores the ramifications. Some other changes as well, but that is the 'big' one.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9704180/1/][I'm Still Here]]/ is really long, but not much actually happens within the story. It takes something like 200,000 words to progress 3 or 4 days. Her fics are well written but tend to read like guilty pleasures and have their fair share of trope use. I still enjoy them though so I put it in there.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11157943/1/][I Still Haven't Found What I'm Looking For]]/ also by Kathryn, is a Star Wars cross over where Harry is an immortal just going about his business in the Star Wars universe.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4745329/1/][On the Way to Greatness]]/ this story stays true to canon as far as houses/people/powers go. It is considered by many to be the best Harry in Slytherin fic there is, but it is pretty popular so you most likely have read it.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/3766574/1/][Prince of the Dark Kingdom]]/ is massively AU. A Voldemort won divergence story, it turns into a Mentor!Voldemort story. Voldemort is still a sociopath in this, but he's not insane and it shows.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8501689/1/][The Havoc Side of the Force]]/ is another Harry in the Star Wars universe story, I prefer this one as it doesn't seem to be as much of a power wank.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9774121/1/][The Legacy]]/ is a marriage contract story that lacks nearly all of the cliches used within that genre. The relationship itself isn't the focus of the story, they don't even officially start dating until 700,000 words in. The problem with this story is that it's unnecessarily verbose, you could probably cut out half of it and have it read the same.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12740667/1/][The Mind Arts]]/ has a Harry who is a prodigy at magic with a special emphasis on mind magic. There's not much I can say without spoiling the story, but if you've read and enjoyed /Harry Potter and the Boy Who Lived/, then you'll most likely like this story as well.

/[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11261838/1/][The Unwinding Golden Thread]]/ has Harry being transported back in time where he ends up attending Hogwarts with Tom Riddle. This story's focus is more on the relationship(platonic) between the two, but there is a major divergence that occurs because of Harry's interference. The Carnivorous Muffin is my favorite author by far.

and I'll type up a little more summary for the rest when I have more time
