** Since I just linked it a couple minutes ago, I suppose I'll link it again. Reign of the Serpent, linkffn(9783012) is probably the most unique and well fleshed out AU I've ever read. It's a shame that it was abandoned.

Grey Space linkao3(12424344) is unique in that it showcases what Harry's life could've been like if Hogwarts started later (14 instead of 11).

The Daring Win linkffn(12005755) is an AU with Harry raised by Doloros Umbridge. It's really, really well done.

And The Wolves All Cry linkffn(8809533) an AU where nobody heard the prophecy. A longish dystopian oneshot, it scratched an itch I didn't realize I had.

Just A Random Tuesday linkffn(3124159) follow Minerva around for a day during Harry's fifth year.
