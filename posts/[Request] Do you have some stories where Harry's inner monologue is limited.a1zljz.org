** I think any fic that is not told from Harry's POV (whether Harry exists or not) should qualify, such as:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10871795/1/A-Little-Child-Shall-Lead-Them][A Little Child Shall Lead Them]], linkffn(10871795)

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5492188/1/Doubting-Thomas][Doubting Thomas]], linkffn(5492188)

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5677867/1/Ginny-Weasley-and-the-Half-Blood-Prince][Ginny Weasley and the Half-Blood Prince]], linkffn(5677867): This fic is fully canon compliant, and Harry is very much a main character. But we never get to see Harry's inner thoughts.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12614436/1/Hermione-Granger-Demonologist][Hermione Granger, Demonologist]], linkffn(12614436)

[[https://www.tthfanfic.org/Story-30822][Hermione Granger and the Boy Who Lived]]: This one is very Trio centric, and Harry is very much involved, but is only told from Hermione's POV.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11419408/1/Not-From-Others][Not From Others]], linkffn(11419408)

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/13052802/1/Petunia-Evans-Tomb-Raider][Petunia Evans, Tomb Raider]], linkffn(13052802): Harry is again listed as a main character, but we do not get to see his POV.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6331126/1/Strangers-at-Drakeshaugh][Strangers at Drakeshaugh]], linkffn(6331126): Harry/Ginny and Ron/Hermione are listed as main characters, but we only get to see their POVs on rare occasions. Most of the narratives comes from Harry's Muggle neighbor (an OC).

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6919395/1/The-Changeling][The Changeling]], linkffn(6919395): All seven years are told from Slytherin!Ginny's POV.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11773877/1/The-Dark-Lord-Never-Died][The Dark Lord Never Died]], linkffn(11773877): Ron, Hermione, Lucius Malfoy, and Dumbledore are POV characters. Harry and Ginny do play important roles, but do not have POV moments.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4356667/1/Wish-Carefully][Wish Carefully]], linkffn(4356667): Narrated from Lucius Malfoy's first person perspective, very ironic, very satisfying.
