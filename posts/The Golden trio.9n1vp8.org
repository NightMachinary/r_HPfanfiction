So I came across "Forging the sword" which heavily centers on Harry, Hermione and Ron as a team after Ginny dies in second year and tbh it's pretty amazing. I adored it, was hooked on constantly and it was something fresh and original. So I tried to find fics similar to it and all I got was basically "Harry x Ginny and Hermione x Ron" after I filtered on [[https://fanfiction.net][fanfiction.net]], which incredibly frustrates me. If you know of any similar fanfiction where their friendship is explored, do let me know. It would be preferable if there was no Harry x Ginny and Hermione x Ron and no Harry x Hermione obviously, but if there is I might bear with it. Thank you very much in advance.
