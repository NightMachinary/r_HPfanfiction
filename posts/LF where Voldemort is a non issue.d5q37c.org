To clarify, I want fics where the focus is not on Voldemort and he is easily dealt with.


** Time travels that don't bother rehashing canon stations generally deal with Voldemort quickly and efficiently:

[[https://m.fanfiction.net/s/10871795/1/][A Little Child Shall Lead Them]], linkffn(10871795): 20 years old Hermione sends her memories and powers back to her 2 years old self. She tells Dumbledore everything and he takes it from there. Two months later, Voldemort and all inner circle Death Eaters are finished.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4740107/1/][Ginny Returns]], linkffn(4740107): 21 years old Ginny returns to her 12 years old self. She doesn't tell Dumbledore, and finishes Voldemort and Co efficiently by herself.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4692717/1/][Many Thanks]], linkffn(4692717): James Potter watches how a time traveling Hermione finishes off Voldemort.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10687059/1/][Returning to the Start]], linkffn(10687059): 21 years old Harry is thrown back to his 11 years old self. He dispatches Voldemort through some Chinese rituals before getting to Hogwarts.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4655545/1/][Reunion]], linkffn(4655545): Deranged and traumatized Harry, Hermione, Luna, and Susan time travel to their 8 years old selves. They kill off most of their enemies and imprison Voldemort before even returning to Hogwarts.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9561455/1/][Spectrum]], linkffn(9561455): A Hermione who has lost everyone time travels to 1935 and kills Tom Riddle right in his orphanage.
