** Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality) is the most controversial fanfiction ever, but if you are okay with an absurdly adult Harry it's quite well written. It's the only fic I've read where the possibilities of using a time turner are explored in more detail.

Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Rune Stone Path) I liked too. The Harry here is less unrealistically smart everywhere and only a savant in one specific area which makes him OP but not unbearable.

Linkffn(The Arithmancer) has super Hermione. I honestly prefer her over super Harry because I feel like it's a smaller difference from canon.

Linkffn(Always read the fine print ; Harry Potter and the unforgivable Tournament ; Inspected by No. 13) are a few short fics where Harry finds some way to become absurdly powerful to rush through the Triwizard.

Linkffn(Blindness by AngelaStarCat) has a super Harry who doesn't attend Hogwarts and study magic by himself. It's a good Harry / Hermione story.

Linkffn(The Mind Arts) has a natural legilimens Harry.

Linkffn(The Many Deaths of Harry Potter) has a Harry with knowledge of the future until his next Death. He does become absurdly powerful.

Linkffn(If looks could kill) has a Parselmouth Harry who uses his talent. The Death Eaters have no chance.

Linkffn(Harry Potter and the power of paranoia) has Harry becoming pretty powerful without doing much in the end.

Linkffn(Seventh Horcrux) Voldemort Harry. Obviously absurd.

Linkffn(Honestly, Harry!) has Harry messing around with OP spells.

Linkffn(Poison Pen) Harry wins by writing letters.

Linkffn(Runic Animagi) Phoenix Harry.

Linkffn(Where in the world is Harry Potter?) super Harry messing around after defeating Voldemort.

Linkffn(Harry gets motivated) absurdly strong motivation results in absurdly strong Harry. Voldemort is preventing him from learning effectively so he has to go.
