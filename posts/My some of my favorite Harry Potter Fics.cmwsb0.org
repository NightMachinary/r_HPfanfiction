Favorites of mine:

-Linkffn(The Many Deaths of Harry Potter)

Scary Voldemort VS a Harry who takes things seriously and quickly becomes more powerful.

-Linkffn(Hermione Granger, Demonologist)

One of my favorite Slytherin!Hermione stories. She invites one of the kings of Hell to tea, because she can.

-Linkffn(Divided and Entwined)

Nazi!Death Eaters, "Appeasement Politics", "Don't buy from mudbloods": After some disturbing parallels to 1930s Germany, Hermione decides to organize a resistance and fight back. Brutal war fic.

-Linkffn(Harry Potter and the Lady Thief)

Badass revenge, competent auror!Harry VS catburglar!Hermione. Great action scenes.

-Linkffn(The Arithmancer)

Nice worldbuilding with wizarding academia, creative spellcrafting, and fascinating math. If you are a math and physics nerd like me, you'll love it.

-Linkffn(New Blood)

Not completed yet, but looking very promising so far. Hermione is slowly convincing the entire magical world that she's the purest pureblood in all of Britain, scams other Slytherin girls out of their money with overpriced MLM makeup, and convinces both Harry and Voldemort to trust her.
