For the unfamiliar: a harem comedy is /not/ the same as Harry!Harem; the focus isn't on wish fulfillment, it's on hijinks. Imagine a cross between The Poker Game by Enterprise1701-d and A Black Comedy by nonjon, and you're in the right ballpark.

The characters involved aren't important, resolution isn't important, I'm just interested in laughing.
