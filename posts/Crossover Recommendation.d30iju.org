:Author: SneakyDeaners
:Score: 10
:DateUnix: 1568251375.0
:DateShort: 2019-Sep-12
:END:
Any stories similar in style to linkffn([[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8501689/1/The-Havoc-side-of-the-Force]] ) that anyone can recommend? I just finished reading this, as well as linkffn([[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11157943/1/I-Still-Haven-t-Found-What-I-m-Looking-For]] ) and really enjoyed the idea of Harry being placed into a new universe and immediately start screwing up canon. I am looking for just about any type of crossover where Harry goes to another series, whether its Star Wars, Naruto, or Avengers, or whatever. Most stories are fine, single pairing, no pairing, harem (within reason), and preferably where Harry is strong, not pre-Hogwarts Harry getting dumped into a new universe and filling in the role of the main character. The only things I don't like are yaoi stories, and to at least make sure that the crossover somewhat makes sense of how it happens, even if it is taking the first thousand words to describe Harry being tossed through a portal.

A good example of this would be linkffn([[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12772385/1/FILFY-teacher]]) where it describes how the Harry Potter universe and the Highschool DxD one are connected.

Thank you!
