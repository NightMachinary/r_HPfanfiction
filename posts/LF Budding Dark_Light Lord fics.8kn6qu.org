Don't care if it's Harry, Ron, Hermione, Voldemort, Dumbledore, or anyone else for that matter, as long as the fanfic centers on the rise of a Lord/Lady. Kind of like [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9238861/1/Applied-Cultural-Anthropology-or][Applied Cultural Anthropology]] or something of an extensive lore on Lords like [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/895946/Lightning-on-the-Wave][Lightning on the Wave's]] fanfic saga. While a bit of romance is okay, please no Lord Potter who-somehow-gets-everything-instantly-via-inheritance-or-some-such-shit. PLEASE also no harems. Thanks.
