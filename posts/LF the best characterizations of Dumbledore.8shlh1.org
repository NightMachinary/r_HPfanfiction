#+TITLE: LF the best characterizations of Dumbledore

* LF the best characterizations of Dumbledore
:PROPERTIES:
:Score: 4
:DateUnix: 1529494663.0
:DateShort: 2018-Jun-20
:FlairText: Request
:END:
Rarely do I come across a Dumbledore that is even similar to his Canon self. I want a Dumbledore that's just as awesome as his Canon persona, where he isn't an 'old fool' and very competent with magic, a genius and most of all, /retains his eccentric and whimsy personality/ with his quirky sense of humor.


** Lets see:

- The One He Feared has been mentioned already (nice job taure)
- linkffn(What You Leave Behind): Dumbledore is used sparingly, but his characterization is frankly spot on in this fic
- Realignment linkffn(Realignment): at one point he says to Tom Riddle that he should eat more candy now, while he is still young and hearty. Tom actually takes him up on this.
- linkffn(The Amplitude, Frequency, and Resistance of the Soul Bond): this one is somewhat crack, but it is /the whimsiest Dumbledore/ I have ever come across. At one point he joins in a snowball fight by animating a bunch of snowmen to throw snowballs at the kids. As a bonus, McGonagall creates a snow dragon that eats the snowmen.
- linkffn(Magicks of the Arcane): the best Dumbledore stories tend to also be mentor!Dumbledore stories as well. His characterization here is variable, but at one point he and Harry get drunk during the Yule Ball and start tearing the place up. Plus a lemon drop teleportation device is always fun.
- Getting Answers linkffn(Getting Answers): next-gen time travel, at one point James Sirius Potter turns his robes tie-die, and Dumbledore asks if he can keep them (he did live through the 70s).
- Also, not as whimsical but linkffn(By the Divining Light; Conlaodh's Song) contain a lot of Dumbledore as Harry's mentor. There are good moments; at one point Harry and Dumbledore go parachuting into a magic realm (seriously).
