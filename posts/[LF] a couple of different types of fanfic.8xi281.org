** u/InquisitorCOC:
#+begin_quote
  1.) Harry is raised by good people
#+end_quote

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/5904185/1/Emperor][Emperor]], linkffn(5904185): Raised by his own parents, he would eventually become a warlord and tyrant. Best war epic among HP fanfics.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/9863146/1/The-Accidental-Animagus][The Accidental Animagus]] and its sequel [[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12088294/1/Animagus-at-War][Animagus at War]], linkffn(9863146;12088294): Raised by the Grangers, he and Hermione became siblings. Dumbledore was also very happy and soon brought Tonkses to help them out. Harry entered Hogwarts with a large support base already.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/11773877/1/The-Dark-Lord-Never-Died][The Dark Lord Never Died]], linkffn(11773877): Raised by Sirius in France and later trained by Dumbledore personally; extreme AU and my favorite Ron/Hermione romance; Dumbledore was one BAMF.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12005755/1/The-Daring-Win][The Daring Win]], linkffn(12005755), is very different as Harry was raised by Dolores Umbridge!

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/6635363/1/When-In-Doubt-Obliviate][When In Doubt, Obliviate]], linkffn(6635363): Lockhart found and raised Harry. He became an expert of memory charm early on and was very cunning. Very enjoyable story.
