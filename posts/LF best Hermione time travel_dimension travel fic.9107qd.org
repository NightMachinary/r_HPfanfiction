I have read Reverse by Lady Moonglow and it was phenomenal, and I'm crying internally bc it's not complete yet. I've read stories about Hermione using the time turner but instead of jumping back into her timeline she jumps into another dimension with the same flow of events as hers, most explained it as dimension travel so that Hermione's original timeline wasn't really affected by the changes she can do in the past as she jumped into another timeline instead of hers. So her original timeline continues to flow without her presence. Does that make sense? I'd like to read those too, if someone had the pleasure of collecting those, at least decently written, I'm okay with WIPs. Just strictly no Ron/Hermione pairing (honestly why do people ship Ronmione is beyond me)

As for time-travel without the complications of the things mentioned above, any era would do, I like angst but I love well-written thought-out plots more. I do have a liking to Tomione (I can't seem to like Volmione tho) and Remione. But any pairings would do! Especially if it's a favorite fic of yours just recommend away! Just no Snape/Hermione (unless it's phenomenal like the ones Hermione was sent and reborn as a phoenix and it being their animagus forms and they semi-shift with Remus in wolf form and Hermione Ankaa Black yeah that one) but strictly no Ron/Hermione pairings.

Thanks! Your recommendation is appreciated!
