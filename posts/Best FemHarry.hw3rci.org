* Best Fem!Harry?

I know this has probably been asked a thousand times, so sorry! I'm new to HP fanfiction, and I'm looking for something good to read.

I would like it if she wasn't a carbon copy of Harry. I want her to have her own personality.

I would prefer if it didn't follow canon 100%, but not a complete AU.

No Slytherin Fem!Harry, please.

If there are pairings, I'd prefer them *NOT to be with Draco, Snape, Voldemort/Tom, or anyone who could be the age of her father.*

Also, I would prefer it if there was no romance until at least book 4. Age 14 is still pushing it a little bit IMO, but I'm fine if it's not a super serious relationship at this age.

I want it to take place in the same timeline as the original, as well.

No slash.

40k+

Please no dead fics either.
