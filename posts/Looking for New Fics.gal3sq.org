** Time to list some personal favorites. Fraid the only fic I am familiar with that you shared was Grow Young with Me, which is a wonderful fic.

Make A Wish - linkffn(2318355) - An absolutely amazing story that is super fun. Also, anything by [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/686093/Rorschach-s-Blot][Rorschach's Blot]] is a good read. I strongly recommend checking his stories out.

Picnic Panic - linkffn(12265183) - Most touching fic in my opinion and one that is always going to stay with me. Has Harry acting fatherly which is always great.

Dancing in the Green - linkffn(11703507) - It's a very good fourth-year story, strongly recommend.

Ice Cream - linkffn(13323518) - This one is cute and I like to share it.

Daphne Greengrass and the Importance of Intent - linkffn(13133746) - One of the best Harry and Daphne fics out there and has good read and reread quality. Its sequel is just as good.

Inspected by No 13 - linkffn(10485934) - A good fun crack-fic that is well done. One to be shared with others for sure

From Southampton to New York and Beyond - linkffn(12300252) is another great story and the author [[https://www.fanfiction.net/u/8431550/Dorothea-Greengrass][Dorothea Greengrass]] a good few other interesting stories to read.

Novocaine - linkffn(13022013) - A well thought out and interesting world-building story that also delves more into the magical culture.

The Little Child to Lead Him - linkffn(3123443) - A lovely story about Harry becoming a father.

I think I call this list my must-reads for anyone new to the fandom. If you like any of these feel free to PM me for more recommendations. Always happy to help point someone to good stories.
