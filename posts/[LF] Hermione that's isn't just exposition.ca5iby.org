A Harry-centric fic where Hermione's role as exposition is diminished. I want her contributing a different dynamic to the group. She could be a spell maker, a researcher, just not providing information in the background about events. No bashing of anyone who isn't a death eater it Umbridge. Edit: I'd prefer Harry/Hermione be avoided, as I would prefer Harry taking Hermione to task avoided.

Hermione gets expelled from Hogwarts and does her own thing in those two while Harry and Ron stay at Hogwarts and train for the fight against Voldemort.
