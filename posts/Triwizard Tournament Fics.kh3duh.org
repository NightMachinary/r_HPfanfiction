I really enjoy the concept of the Triwizard Tournament and would like to read some fanfics focusing on it. I don't care if it's GoF AR or about an entirely different tournament like in the past or next gen.

It should not be too romance heavy, I don't mind a little bit but it shouldn't be the main focus. I also don't like heavy character bashing. It should not involve any TT. If it's a GoF AR please no dark!harry or dark!hermione. OCs are okay as long as they are not Mary Sues.
