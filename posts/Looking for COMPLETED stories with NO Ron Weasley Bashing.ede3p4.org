Sometimes they don't even have the Weasley bashing in the summary or the A/N so it comes off as a surprise, and I hate it.

So, I'm looking for any and all completed stories that you guys have enjoyed that have no Ron Weasley or NO Weasley bashing in general.

Can be any pairing, set in Hogwarts, before or after it. Can be as canon or as AU as possible.
