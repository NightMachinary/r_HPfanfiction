JKR somehow managed to avoid them interacting in canon (excluding where Ron yells at Voldemort after Harry 'dies' in DH). Anybody?


** This is surprisingly rare. Even among very Hermione centric authors (Starfox5, White Squirrel), direct confrontations between them generally remain that of combat, not dialogues.

I can only think of these fics in which Hermione (and/or Ron) has spoken directly with Voldemort:

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/12614436/1/Hermione-Granger-Demonologist][Hermione Granger, Demonologist]], linkffn(12614436): Hermione and Voldemort are allies

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/4692717/1/Many-Thanks][Many Thanks]], linkffn(4692717): Time traveling Hermione and Voldemort had a brief conversation before their end fight.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/2746577/1/Resistance][Resistance]], linkffn(2746577): This one has several Hermione vs Voldemort dialogues.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/10677106/1/Seventh-Horcrux][Seventh Hocrux]], linkffn(10677106): I admit I'm cheating a little here, but this 'Harry' thinks like Voldemort, acts like Voldemort, and has the power of Voldemort.

[[https://www.fanfiction.net/s/8192853/1/Yes-I-am-Harry-s-Brother][Yes, I am Harry's Brother]], linkffn(8192853): Hermione had a crush on him for a while.
