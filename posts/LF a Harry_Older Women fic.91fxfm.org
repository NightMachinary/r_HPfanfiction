***** True. I suppose I'm just not in the mood for it now, but Summer of Change has been in my list of guilty pleasures for a while, so I can understand the feeling.

In truth, the thing that bothered me the most is the 'Tonks is impressed' part, even if it's just a few tiny words. It's really symptomatic of an author who wants to have a super OP and impressive MC but can't be bothered to think up actually impressive things for them to know or do. So they just go around having secondary characters and randos being amazed by the MC breathing or something.

I guess I can appreciate an OP MC powerwank as much as anyone, but it has to be done in such a way that the world around them isn't comprised of cardboard cutouts with big round and admiring eyes. I mean, if a sharp wit, confident poise and machiavellian cunning are only ever used to cut through strawmen and outsmart mentally deficient 3-year-olds, I really don't see the point. An OP character is a terrible thing to waste.

Anyway, rant finished ;D
