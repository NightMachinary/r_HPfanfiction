#+TITLE: What are the best "War Harry" fics?

* What are the best "War Harry" fics?
:PROPERTIES:
:Score: 28
:DateUnix: 1586502507.0
:DateShort: 2020-Apr-10
:FlairText: Request
:END:
Wether it be Harry Potter, man alone, a magical Churchill unyieldingly leading his country to victory in the face of impossible odds, to Hary Potter, emotionless killer, undercover operator, to field commander of the order of the phoenix, I want to see Harry Potter in a war-torn crisis. In the grimdark present of the magical war, there is one figure that stands tall, unyielding to the forces of darkness (or other forces of darkness if it is a lol!Evil!Dark!Harry). And that, is Harry Potter.
