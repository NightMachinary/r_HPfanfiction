I wanna see fics where the MC snipes the death eaters from afar, dousing the manor with toxin gasses, maybe calling the pest exterminator against nagini? In almost every fic I have ever read, the fight scenes are one on one dueling to the death. Why the hell doesn't Harry and co. doesn't just drop a bomb they pimped out(Penetrating the wards if they have any, more firepower etc...) from a helicopter? I just don't want to read scenes where Harry goes charging against Voldie like a fucking idiot. Too much Leeroy Jenkins.

They no longer bothered with gallant duels, but sought to get into tactical advantageous positions to deliver maximum firepower against their enemies. Voldemort himself however, was leagues above his minions and required some 'Hero's Luck' to defeat.
