** Here are my top 3 R/Hr fics:

[[https://m.fanfiction.net/s/11773877/1/The-Dark-Lord-Never-Died][The Dark Lord Never Died]], linkffn(11773877): Major AU. Lucius Malfoy took some risk and won big after Halloween 1981.
