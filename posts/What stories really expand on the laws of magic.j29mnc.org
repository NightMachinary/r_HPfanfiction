#+TITLE: What stories really expand on the laws of magic?

* What stories really expand on the laws of magic?
:PROPERTIES:
:Author: Driptoe
:Score: 3
:DateUnix: 1601418151.0
:DateShort: 2020-Sep-30
:FlairText: Request
:END:
I very much like the hard magic system in the worlds of Avatar and Fullmetal Alchemist, so I'd love a story where the limits of magic in the wizarding world are explored! Does anyone have a good fic to scratch that itch?


** Victoria Potter pushes the boundaries of Rowling's magic everywhere, among other things. I would not, however, describe it as a hard magic system.

NoahPhantom's Albus Potter series takes Wizarding magic to an extreme.

Pureblood Pretense does this for certain areas of magic, and certainly introduces harder elements to the system.

The Phoenix and the Serpent was written before book 5 was published, and has a bunch of divergences that really make sense in that context. The magic introduced in this fic is very creative and feels like a natural direction the series could have gone.

All four of these are also really good reads, so the magic is not the only appeal.
