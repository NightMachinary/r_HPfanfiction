It can be a power, it can be his intelligence, it can be an identity. It can be dark harry or not. It can be harry joins voldemort, it can be harry time travel from the future. It can be Harry is dumbledore, possession, game harry, PTSD.

I don't really care what it is, I just want harry to have a secret he's hiding from everyone around him and dealing having to keep it hidden and eventually the consequences of having it found out.

I would like well-written fics as little edgy and with as little bashing as possible, either still being written or completed. They can be abandoned if they are insanely good too or if it doesn't leave me unsatisfied.

Graphic details don't bother me. but I'd rather avoid sex. I don't want erotica or romance, so nothing with sex and pairing being the central point of the story.

I know it's weirdly specific, but I'm hungry to read fics and I'd really like to see what you guys throw at me.
